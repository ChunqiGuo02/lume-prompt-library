# 正文 prompt
A polished Japanese promotional poster for a community and sustainability project called 【セカイムラ】, designed like a premium eco-lifestyle campaign flyer. Vertical layout, clean and elegant, with a soft cream paper background and a luminous, aspirational mood. The top half is dominated by a giant translucent globe centered in the composition, glowing with warm sunrise light from the upper right. Inside the globe is a breathtaking aerial landscape of a green mountain valley opening to a calm blue bay or lake, with tiny villages and fields below, cinematic and utopian. Over the globe, place a smaller Japanese slogan line reading 【世界は1つの村になる。】 and beneath it the large main title in bold dark navy Japanese calligraphic lettering: 【セカイムラ】. Around the globe, create a collage of exactly 8 candid lifestyle photos fading into the edges: 1) top left, two people in conversation outdoors under trees; 2) upper left-mid, several people working together in a rice paddy; 3) left middle, a circle gathering inside a rustic wooden structure at night; 4) lower left, two portrait-style figures in nature; 5) top right, a group sharing a meal at a long table in warm indoor light; 6) right middle, a person tending plants or crops; 7) right lower-mid, a line of people holding hands on a ridge at sunset; 8) lower right, a close portrait of a person eating or holding food. The photographic style should feel human, communal, slightly idealized, with soft depth of field, natural skin tones, golden-hour highlights, and smooth ad-poster compositing. The bottom half contains 3 evenly spaced feature cards on a light cream background, separated by subtle vertical dividers. Card 1 on the left has the heading "セカイムラ 新月会" in dark blue, a circular photo of a nighttime campfire circle, and a short block of Japanese descriptive text below. Card 2 in the center has the heading "セカイムラ 満月会" in warm gold, a circular photo of an indoor evening gathering under string lights, decorative radiating sunburst lines around the circle, and a short block of Japanese descriptive text below. Card 3 on the right has the heading "セカイムラ 米部が今の一押し" in green, small rice plant illustrations near the title, a circular photo of a wooden bowl of white rice, and a short block of Japanese descriptive text below. Under these cards, add a centered Japanese statement line in dark blue: 【人・自然・文化がつながり、循環する未来を一緒につくる。】. Beneath that, place 4 small icon-and-text items in a horizontal row, each with a simple thin-line icon and two-line Japanese label: 1) people icon with "つながる 仲間と出会える"; 2) house icon with "まなぶ 暮らしを学べる"; 3) sprout icon with "つくる 未来を共につくる"; 4) globe icon with "ひろがる 世界にひろげる". At the very bottom center, include a circular logo mark followed by the project name in Japanese, and underneath a website URL: 【www.sekaimura.com】. Add a small QR code at the bottom right with tiny Japanese text indicating details are on the website. Use a refined palette of deep navy, forest green, gold, cream, and sky blue. Overall style: high-end Japanese nonprofit poster, eco-community branding, inspirational, harmonious, photoreal collage with subtle graphic design elements, balanced typography, soft glows, and magazine-quality print finish.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
一张精美的日本社区与可持续发展项目宣传海报，项目名称为 【セカイムラ】，设计风格如同高端生态生活方式宣传单。垂直布局，简洁优雅，采用柔和的米色纸张背景，营造出明亮且充满愿景的氛围。上半部分以构图中心的一个巨大半透明地球仪为主体，右上方散发出温暖的日出光芒。地球仪内部呈现出一幅令人惊叹的绿色山谷航拍景观，山谷通向宁静的蓝色海湾或湖泊，下方点缀着村庄和农田，极具电影感和乌托邦色彩。在地球仪上方，放置一行较小的日文标语 【世界は1つの村になる。】，下方是加粗的深海军蓝日文书法字体主标题：【セカイムラ】。围绕地球仪，拼贴 8 张自然融入边缘的生活照：1) 左上，两人在树下户外交谈；2) 左中上，几人在稻田里共同劳作；3) 左中，夜晚在质朴木结构建筑内围坐；4) 左下，自然环境中的两人肖像；5) 右上，一群人在温暖的室内灯光下长桌聚餐；6) 右中，一人在照料植物或农作物；7) 右中下，夕阳下人们在山脊上牵手排成一列；8) 右下，一人正在进食或手持食物的特写肖像。摄影风格应体现人文感、社区感，略带理想化，具有柔和的景深、自然的肤色、黄金时刻的高光以及平滑的广告海报合成效果。下半部分在浅米色背景上包含 3 张间距均匀的功能卡片，由细微的垂直分隔线隔开。左侧卡片 1 标题为深蓝色的“セカイムラ 新月会”，配有夜间篝火圆圈照片及一段简短的日文描述文本。中间卡片 2 标题为暖金色的“セカイムラ 満月会”，配有灯串下室内晚间聚会的圆形照片，圆圈周围有装饰性的放射状太阳光线，下方附带简短的日文描述文本。右侧卡片 3 标题为绿色的“セカイムラ 米部が今の一押し”，标题旁有小型稻苗插画，配有一碗白米饭的圆形照片及一段简短的日文描述文本。在这些卡片下方，添加一行居中的深蓝色日文声明：【人・自然・文化がつながり、循環する未来を一緒につくる。】。在此下方，横向排列 4 个小型图标及文字项，每个图标配有简单的细线条图标和两行日文标签：1) 人物图标配“つながる 仲間と出会える”；2) 房屋图标配“まなぶ 暮らしを学べる”；3) 新芽图标配“つくる 未来を共につくる”；4) 地球图标配“ひろがる 世界にひろげる”。最底部中心处包含一个圆形徽标，后接日文项目名称，下方为网站 URL：【www.sekaimura.com】。右下角添加一个小型二维码，并附带微小的日文说明，提示详情请见网站。采用深海军蓝、森林绿、金色、米色和天蓝色的精致配色方案。整体风格：高端日本非营利组织海报、生态社区品牌设计、鼓舞人心、和谐、带有微妙图形设计元素的写实拼贴、平衡的排版、柔和的光晕以及杂志级的印刷质感。
