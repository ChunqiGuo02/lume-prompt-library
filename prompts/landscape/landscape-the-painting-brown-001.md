# 正文 prompt
A surreal oil painting in the style of Leonardo da Vinci's Mona Lisa, reimagined as a teddy-bear hybrid portrait. The subject sits in the same half-length frontal pose with folded hands, wearing the dark Renaissance dress and soft draped shawl of the original painting, against the classic hazy landscape with winding water, distant mountains, and muted atmospheric perspective. The figure has 2 round fluffy teddy bear ears on top of the head and 2 plush furry paws folded at the bottom, with a warm beige teddy-bear fur chest replacing the visible skin of the neckline and hands. Long dark brown hair falls smoothly over both shoulders. The face area is intentionally left as a flat featureless brown rectangular block, creating an uncanny censored or unfinished effect. Use cracked varnish texture, aged museum-painting patina, smoky sfumato transitions, low-saturation brown and olive tones, soft golden lighting, centered composition, highly detailed fur texture blended into classical portrait brushwork, whimsical yet eerie fine-art parody.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
一幅模仿列奥纳多·达·芬奇《蒙娜丽莎》风格的超现实主义油画，将其重新构想为泰迪熊混合肖像。主体保持了原作中半身正面、双手交叠的姿势，身着文艺复兴时期的深色长裙和柔软的披肩，背景是经典的朦胧风景，包含蜿蜒的水流、远山以及柔和的空气透视感。人物头顶长有 2 只圆润蓬松的泰迪熊耳朵，底部交叠着 2 只毛茸茸的爪子，领口和手部原本的皮肤由温暖的米色泰迪熊毛皮取代。深棕色的长发顺滑地垂在双肩。面部区域特意留白为一个平坦且无特征的棕色矩形块，营造出一种诡异的被审查或未完成的效果。采用龟裂的清漆质感、陈旧的博物馆画作光泽、烟雾般的晕涂法（sfumato）过渡、低饱和度的棕色和橄榄色调、柔和的金色光线、居中构图，将高度细腻的毛皮质感与古典肖像笔触完美融合，呈现出一种既怪诞又奇妙的艺术戏仿效果。
