# 正文 prompt
Prompt: A surreal miniature winter scene in the mountains. A large blue Pringles can lies tipped over in the snow, spilling potato chips that form a winding snowboard track. Tiny snowboarders ride smoothly along the curved chip-and-snow path, carving through the slope. Snow-covered mountains and clear blue sky in the background. Bright daylight, playful fantasy tone, ultra-realistic textures, crisp snow detail, shallow depth of field.
Motion (Primary):
Mini snowboarders glide downhill in smooth arcs, carving gently along the curved track.
Secondary Motion:
Loose chip pieces tumble slowly in the snow, fine snow particles kick up behind the boards, light powder spray follows turns.
Camera:
Cinematic macro shot, slow tracking follow along the snowboard path, low angle close to the snow surface, gentle parallax as the camera moves forward, stable and smooth.
Style & Lighting:
Bright natural winter daylight, clean contrast, vibrant product colors, soft highlights on snow, realistic shadows, whimsical commercial aesthetic.
Constraints:
Keep Pringles can and logo shape stable and readable.
Snowboarders remain miniature and proportional.
No distortion of chips, no melting snow, no object duplication.
Negative Prompt:
warped logo, unreadable text, stretched can, broken snowboarders, extra limbs, chaotic motion, shaky camera, flicker, excessive motion blur, glitch artifacts.
