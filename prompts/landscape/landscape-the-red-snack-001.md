# 正文 prompt
Create a dramatic square commercial poster for a spicy snack brand, with a fiery red storm atmosphere and high-energy food advertising style. The background is a dark crimson sky filled with glowing clouds, scattered sparks, and visible lightning bolts. In the center, a giant tornado made of swirling transparent wind rings, glossy whole red chili peppers, square corn chips, crumbs, and seasoning particles rises upward from an opened snack bag, forming a powerful vortex. The tornado should feel dynamic and explosive, with chips and chili peppers flying outward in different sizes and depths for a cinematic 3D look. At the bottom center, place a glossy red snack packet standing upright, labeled with the brand logo and product name 【Crispy Nachos Chips】, with a spicy flavor badge reading 【Spicy Masala】, a small chili illustration, a corn note near the bottom, and a printed image of square nacho chips on the front. Put a stylized playful logo at the top left reading 【SnaX】 in bold yellow and white script with a red outline, and a clean white platform watermark at the top right reading 【Pollo.ai】. On the left side, add a huge stacked distressed headline in bold condensed uppercase, with the first word in golden yellow and the remaining three lines in white, reading exactly: 【WARNING! CRUNCH STORM INCOMING】. Use intense rim lighting, glossy highlights, deep shadows, rich red-orange color grading, and a premium snack advertisement composition that feels spicy, crunchy, chaotic, and irresistible.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
为一款辣味零食品牌创作一张极具戏剧性的正方形商业海报，营造火热的红色风暴氛围，展现高能量的食品广告风格。背景为深红色的天空，云层闪烁，火花四溅，并伴有清晰可见的闪电。画面中心，一个由旋转的透明风环、光泽饱满的红辣椒、方形玉米片、碎屑和调味料颗粒组成的巨大龙卷风，从打开的零食袋中向上升腾，形成强劲的涡流。龙卷风应具有动态感和爆发力，薯片和辣椒以不同的大小和深度向外飞散，呈现出电影级的 3D 质感。在底部中心，放置一个直立的光泽红色零食包装袋，上面印有品牌 Logo 和产品名称 【Crispy Nachos Chips】，并配有辣味标签 【Spicy Masala】、一个小辣椒插图、底部的玉米元素以及包装正面印有的方形玉米片图案。在左上角放置一个风格化且俏皮的 Logo 【SnaX】，采用醒目的黄白相间手写体，并带有红色描边；右上角添加一个简洁的白色平台水印 【Pollo.ai】。在左侧，添加一个巨大的堆叠式做旧标题，采用粗体压缩大写字母，第一行文字为金黄色，其余三行文字为白色，内容必须为：【WARNING! CRUNCH STORM INCOMING】。运用强烈的轮廓光、光泽高光、深邃阴影、浓郁的红橙色调，以及高级的零食广告构图，呈现出辛辣、酥脆、混乱且令人无法抗拒的视觉效果。
