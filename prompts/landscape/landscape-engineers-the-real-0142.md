# 正文 prompt
Use case: historical-scene. Create a documentary-style photograph of a mission control room in 1969 at the moment engineers realize a lunar maneuver succeeded. Show rows of consoles, CRT screens, paper checklists, headset cables, ashtrays, coffee cups, and engineers reacting with restrained relief. The scene should feel historically plausible without copying a real copyrighted photo. Atmosphere: fluorescent light, cigarette haze, tense body language, analog equipment, slight film grain. No real agency logos, no famous faces, no watermark.
