# 正文 prompt
A bright, high-resolution outdoor action photograph of a small 【pug】 running toward the camera on a narrow rocky hiking trail through an alpine meadow. The dog is centered slightly low in the frame, mid-stride with one front paw lifted, mouth open, tongue out, ears perked, and an excited expression, wearing a 【navy blue】 chest harness. The trail curves gently back into rolling green hills and is bordered on both sides by dense wildflowers and grasses, including visible clusters of blue, purple, yellow, white, and pink blooms. In the distance, place 3 tiny hikers on the trail for scale. The background features a dramatic panoramic mountain range with 4 prominent snow-capped peaks, rugged rocky faces, and patches of glacier under a clear vivid 【blue】 summer sky with a few thin wispy clouds. Use crisp natural daylight, vibrant but realistic colors, deep depth and clarity from foreground flowers to distant mountains, eye-level low-angle perspective, documentary travel photography style, sharp focus on the running dog, and a joyful adventurous hiking mood.
