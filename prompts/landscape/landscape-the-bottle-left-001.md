# 正文 prompt
Create a vertical luxury skincare advertisement in a soft feminine pastel aesthetic, styled like a viral Instagram or TikTok beauty promo with no app UI. Show a single glass dropper serum bottle centered on a fluffy lavender-purple surface, photographed in ultra-realistic studio lighting with warm golden sunlight streaming from the upper left, soft shadows, glossy reflections, dreamy haze, and creamy bokeh in the background. The bottle label should read 【ELEGANT SKINCARE】 and 【SERUM】, with a pale pink-lilac translucent bottle and shiny rose-gold dropper cap. Keep the composition clean, premium, cozy, glossy, and highly aesthetic, with the serum as the clear focal point.
