# 正文 prompt
Create an elegant luxury travel poster for 【NORTHERN EUROPE】, designed like a premium tourism campaign brochure cover. Use a wide symmetrical layout with a thin gold decorative border and refined editorial typography. At the top center, place a small compass-star icon above the word DISCOVER, then a very large serif headline reading 【NORTHERN EUROPE】 in deep navy blue. Below it, add a gold uppercase tagline: 【BREATHTAKING NATURE. RICH CULTURE. TIMELESS EXPERIENCES.】 and beneath that a smaller elegant italic line: 【Four Countries. Countless Memories.】. The main image area is divided into 4 equal vertical scenic panels showing iconic destinations at golden hour with soft glowing skies and cinematic realism. Panel 1: Denmark, Copenhagen, a colorful harbor waterfront with historic buildings, boats, calm reflective water, and a domed landmark in the background; include a small Danish flag above the labels DENMARK and COPENHAGEN. Panel 2: Sweden, Stockholm, a waterfront old-town skyline with church spires and warm sunset light reflected in the water; include a small Swedish flag above the labels SWEDEN and STOCKHOLM. Panel 3: Finland, Helsinki, a small stone castle-like island fortress in blue water under a bright sky; include a small Finnish flag above the labels FINLAND and HELSINKI. Panel 4: Norway, dramatic fjords with steep jagged mountains, a waterfall, and a tiny red-roof coastal village at the water’s edge; include a small Norwegian flag above the labels NORWAY and FJORDS OF NORWAY. Beneath the 4 country panels, add a row of 4 gold line icons with captions, evenly spaced: mountain icon with ICONIC LANDSCAPES, Viking ship icon with RICH HERITAGE, moose icon with WILD NATURE, camera icon with UNFORGETTABLE JOURNEYS. Near the bottom center, place a gold divider and the title A TASTE OF THE NORTH. Under it, show exactly 5 food thumbnail panels in a horizontal strip, each on a plate or bowl with warm appetizing lighting and dark blue framing: 1) Smørrebrød — Denmark, open-faced sandwich topped with shrimp; 2) Köttbullar — Sweden, Swedish meatballs with cream sauce and lingonberries; 3) Gravad lax — Finland, sliced cured salmon in a bowl; 4) Fish soup — Norway, creamy fish soup with herbs and seafood; 5) Kanelbulle — Sweden, a glossy cinnamon bun on a plate. Use a polished high-end travel-ad aesthetic, photorealistic scenery, soft sunset color grading, navy and gold graphic design, balanced composition, crisp text placement, and a nostalgic upscale European tourism mood.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
创作一张优雅的北欧奢华旅行海报，主题为 【NORTHERN EUROPE】，设计风格参考高端旅游宣传册封面。采用宽幅对称布局，配以纤细的金色装饰边框和精致的编辑排版。在顶部中心位置，放置一个小型罗盘星图标，下方为“DISCOVER”字样，随后是一个非常醒目的衬线字体大标题，内容为 【NORTHERN EUROPE】，颜色采用深海军蓝。在其下方，添加一行金色大写标语：【BREATHTAKING NATURE. RICH CULTURE. TIMELESS EXPERIENCES.】，再下方是一行较小的优雅斜体副标题：【Four Countries. Countless Memories.】。主体图像区域被平均分为 4 个垂直风景板块，展示黄金时段的标志性目的地，呈现柔和的霞光和电影级的写实感。板块 1：丹麦哥本哈根，色彩斑斓的海港码头，背景为历史建筑、船只、平静的倒影水面及圆顶地标；在标签 DENMARK 和 COPENHAGEN 上方添加一面小丹麦国旗。板块 2：瑞典斯德哥尔摩，水滨老城天际线，教堂尖顶在温暖的日落光线下映照在水中；在标签 SWEDEN 和 STOCKHOLM 上方添加一面小瑞典国旗。板块 3：芬兰赫尔辛基，蓝天碧水下的一座小型石砌城堡式岛屿要塞；在标签 FINLAND 和 HELSINKI 上方添加一面小芬兰国旗。板块 4：挪威，壮丽的峡湾，陡峭崎岖的山脉，瀑布，以及水边的小型红顶沿海村庄；在标签 NORWAY 和 FJORDS OF NORWAY 上方添加一面小挪威国旗。在 4 个国家板块下方，添加一行 4 个均匀分布的金色线条图标及说明：山脉图标配 ICONIC LANDSCAPES，维京船图标配 RICH HERITAGE，驼鹿图标配 WILD NATURE，相机图标配 UNFORGETTABLE JOURNEYS。在底部中心附近，放置一个金色分隔符和标题 A TASTE OF THE NORTH。在其下方，横向排列 5 个美食缩略图板块，每个板块展示在盘子或碗中，配以温暖诱人的灯光和深蓝色边框：1) Smørrebrød — 丹麦，铺满虾仁的开放式三明治；2) Köttbullar — 瑞典，配奶油酱和越橘的瑞典肉丸；3) Gravad lax — 芬兰，碗中的腌制三文鱼片；4) Fish soup — 挪威，配香草和海鲜的奶油鱼汤；5) Kanelbulle — 瑞典，盘中光泽诱人的肉桂卷。整体采用精致的高端旅游广告美学，照片级写实风景，柔和的日落色彩分级，海军蓝与金色的平面设计，平衡的构图，清晰的文字排版，营造出怀旧而高端的欧洲旅游氛围。
