# 正文 prompt
Generate a full color Chinese-text manga about this 【OpenAI 研究科学家, 陈博远】 (first picture), who works on improving the text rendering capability of ChatGPT Image 2 model for the upcoming release. (in the background there is boba tea and a banana taped to the wall with a single slice of duct tape). The model can render insanely small Chinese text when he tried generating some detailed and beautiful multilingual infographics handdrawn-style poster about his hometown, 【无锡】 on his computer screen. His hard work pays off and the team was impressed by the absurdly good quality of multilingual text performance of his model, seeing all the languages it can write. When he takes a break with one hand holding his phone, he received a translated text message from Sam Altman on his phone (avatar attached as second picture), asking him to take a look at the rendered multilingual text in an image he just generated to congrat the team, since Sam only knows English. However, make it funny by let Boyuan outrage (typical manga style) at the end by seeing Sam's generated image contains a "稳稳地接住你" phrase at the central location in an otherwise perfectly rendered image that's used to congrat the team, because this sentence has been memed as an unnatural but funny Chinese sentence GPT likes to use on Chinese internet. Boyuan should rage "天呐! 它又学会了接住!" (with teammates as tiny heads on the side, sweating and saying in Chinese"we are working hard to fix it！"). At the very bottom of the manga, add a tiny line of footnote (very tiny)in Chinese that "note: the entire manga, including this footnote and picture in picture, are all generated with gpt image 2 at once without editing or multiple steps。"

Additional Instructions: Use vertical 1440x2560 image layout, with first row about this researcher working hard, second row about his result on 【无锡】 with multiple languages, third row shows the team excitement, fourth row split into left and right where left shows he takes a break and the phone received a message, right panel shows Sam's text message, and fifth row shows Sam's picture and 陈博远's reaction. No narration except for the first row. Avoid Chinese map. All characters should be in manga style. The banana background should only appear in the first panel and the tape should be a single slice of tape, not a cross tape. The banana and tape decoration should be small as a insignificant easter egg for people to find. OpenAI logo shall only appear on 陈博远's cloth, not elsewhere. No mugs in the scene since we already have the boba. Sam should only appear in the text message panel. The entire manga should be appear as a professional photo of a physical page in a manga book. In the lower right most corner of the poster there is a small “极小中文也清晰可读：” with a paragraph of much smaller Chinese that begins with “（此处为极小字号测试）无锡是作者的故乡，所以做了这幅海报，中文总算是修好了。很多年没回家了，好想吃大闸蟹啊！” (ultra small).

## 中文译文（源站提供）
生成一张全彩中文漫画，主角是这位 【OpenAI 研究员，陈博远】（第一张图），他正致力于提升 ChatGPT Image 2 模型在即将发布版本中的文本渲染能力。（背景里有波霸奶茶，墙上贴着一根用单条胶带粘住的香蕉）。当他在电脑屏幕上尝试生成关于他家乡 【无锡】 的手绘风格多语言信息图海报时，模型展现出了渲染极小中文字符的能力。他的努力得到了回报，团队对模型出色的多语言文本表现感到惊叹，看到了它所能书写的各种语言。当他单手拿着手机休息时，收到了 Sam Altman 发来的翻译后的短信（头像见第二张图），Sam 请他查看一张刚生成的祝贺团队的图片中渲染的多语言文本，因为 Sam 只懂英文。然而，请加入幽默元素：在漫画结尾，让博远（以典型的漫画风格）感到抓狂，因为他看到 Sam 生成的图片中央位置赫然写着“稳稳地接住你”这句话，而图片的其他部分渲染得非常完美。因为这句话在中文互联网上已被玩梗，被视为 GPT 喜欢使用的不自然但有趣的中文短语。博远应该愤怒地大喊：“天哪！它又学会‘接住’了！”（旁边是队友们缩小的头像，流着汗说：“我们正在努力修复它！”）。在漫画的最底部，添加一行极小的中文脚注：“注：整幅漫画，包括此脚注和画中画，均由 GPT Image 2 一次性生成，未经编辑或多步处理。”

额外说明：使用 1440x2560 的垂直图像布局。第一行展示研究员努力工作的场景；第二行展示他在 【无锡】 课题上取得的多语言成果；第三行展示团队的兴奋之情；第四行分为左右两部分，左侧展示他休息时手机收到消息，右侧展示 Sam 的短信内容；第五行展示 Sam 的照片和陈博远的反应。除第一行外，不要使用旁白。避免出现中国地图。所有角色应采用漫画风格。香蕉背景仅出现在第一格，胶带应为单条，而非十字交叉。香蕉和胶带装饰应作为彩蛋，小巧且不显眼。OpenAI 标志仅出现在陈博远的衣服上，其他地方不得出现。场景中不要出现马克杯，因为已经有波霸奶茶了。Sam 仅应出现在短信面板中。整幅漫画应呈现为漫画书实物页面的专业照片。在海报的右下角，添加一行小字“超小号中文也能清晰可读：”，并附上一段更小的中文段落，以“（这是超小字体测试）无锡是作者的家乡，所以我制作了这张海报，中文终于修复了。多年未回家，真想吃大闸蟹！”开头（超小号字体）。
