# 正文 prompt
Create a premium vertical fast-food lifestyle advertisement in a polished commercial photography style, designed like a summer campaign poster for McDonald's and McCafé. Show a glamorous young woman with softly blurred, unidentifiable facial features sitting confidently on the hood of a sleek red sports car parked on a winding mountain road at golden hour. She has long wavy auburn hair glowing in warm sunset backlight, wears a dark green satin bomber jacket with gold detailing, a white camisole, high-waisted burgundy pants, and black ankle boots with heels. Her pose is relaxed and stylish, one leg bent and one extended forward, while she holds out a clear plastic cup of iced coffee with a straw and visible McDonald's golden arches branding toward the camera. Scatter red roses across the car hood around her, and place 1 McDonald's paper takeout bag on the right side of the hood. Use a dramatic canyon or hillside background with warm sunlight, soft haze, cinematic depth of field, and a luxurious aspirational road-trip mood. Include floating red flower petals in the air. Compose the layout as a high-end ad with the model centered slightly right and large promotional typography on the left. Add exactly 3 large headline lines in the upper left: "SIP.", "SAVOR.", and "【SUMMER.】" with the first 2 in bold white and the third in bold golden yellow. Below that, add the subheadline: "Refresh your drive with McCafé." with "McCafé" highlighted in yellow. Add exactly 3 circular feature icons stacked vertically on the left with short captions: "PREMIUM McCAFÉ ICED COFFEE", "WORLD FAMOUS FRIES", and "QUALITY YOU CAN TASTE". Place the McDonald's golden arches logo with the text "i'm lovin' it" in the top right. Add a small lower-left tagline reading "Good days start here." with "start here." in yellow. Create a bold yellow bottom banner spanning the width, divided into 3 content areas: left area reads "SUMMER SAVINGS" and "ENJOY EXCLUSIVE OFFERS"; center area includes a McDonald's app icon and the text "DOWNLOAD THE APP FOR DEALS NEAR YOU"; right area reads "FOLLOW US @MCDONALDS" with social icons. Use brand-accurate red and yellow accents, glossy product styling, realistic lighting, sharp editorial retouching, shallow depth of field, luxury commercial ad composition, and mobile-first poster proportions.
