# 正文 prompt
Edit the mountain bike product photo by adding the bike brand text "/FAST BIKES" on the central metallic frame tube. Place the text on the down tube within the main frame triangle, following the angle and perspective of the bike frame. The lettering should look like real factory-applied branding: clean, durable, slightly glossy, and aligned with the metal. Preserve the bike geometry, tires, trail, dust, bokeh trees, lighting, and shallow depth of field. Do not add any other logos, text, stickers, or watermark.
