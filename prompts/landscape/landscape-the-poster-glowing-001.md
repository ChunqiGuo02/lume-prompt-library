# 正文 prompt
A cinematic vertical poster of 【Sam Altman】 styled like a futuristic football superstar campaign image, waist-up portrait, centered, arms crossed, wearing a plain dark navy or black crewneck sweater, confident and calm posture, looking slightly off-camera. Place him inside a dramatic high-tech stadium or arena at night with bright floodlights glowing from the upper left, dark steel architecture overhead, and a cool blue sci-fi atmosphere. Add floating particles and light debris in the air for energy and motion. Surround the subject with futuristic AI interface graphics: 1 glowing wireframe cube icon on the left side, 1 luminous digital brain or neural-network globe on the right side, and multiple transparent holographic dashboard screens with charts and data panels behind him. Use a deep blue, black, and white color palette with sharp contrast, glossy highlights, subtle lens glow, and premium blockbuster poster lighting. Make the composition sleek, aspirational, and tech-forward, like a sports ad merged with artificial intelligence branding. At the bottom, add the title text 【THE FUTURE】 in very large metallic white uppercase letters, with the word "THE" smaller above the main word, and add the tagline 【Built with Intelligence】 below in smaller spaced-out sans-serif text. Include faint hexagonal HUD linework near the lower corners and a glowing horizontal light streak cutting through the title area. Ultra-detailed, photorealistic, poster design, polished commercial key art, 9:16 aspect ratio.
