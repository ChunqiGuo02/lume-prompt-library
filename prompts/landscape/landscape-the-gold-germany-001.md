# 正文 prompt
Create a luxurious vintage-style travel poster for 【Germany】, designed like a premium tourism campaign advertisement. Use a grand, symmetrical composition with elegant editorial typography and a refined gold border frame with tiny ornamental corner details. At the top center, place a small gold national emblem above the word “DISCOVER”, then a very large serif title reading 【GERMANY】 in deep navy blue. Under it, add the slogan “LAND OF HISTORY. CULTURE. INSPIRATION.” in gold uppercase, followed by a smaller classic serif tagline reading “Timeless Traditions. Unforgettable Journeys.” with a tiny horizontal accent in the colors black, red, and gold.

Show a majestic panoramic montage of iconic German scenery at golden hour under a softly glowing blue sky with scattered clouds. On the left, feature a fairy-tale white hilltop castle resembling Neuschwanstein, perched above autumn trees. In the center distance, place dramatic alpine mountains. On the right, show a historic European riverside city with dense red-roofed houses, an arched stone bridge crossing the water, a domed cathedral, and a towering Gothic cathedral with twin spires. Add calm river reflections and a small white sightseeing boat at the lower right. The image should feel nostalgic, romantic, and aspirational, with warm sunlight, crisp architectural detail, and rich cinematic color grading.

Along the lower middle, include exactly 5 gold line-art cultural icons with labels beneath them, evenly spaced in a horizontal row: “ICONIC LANDMARKS”, “RICH HERITAGE”, “VIBRANT CULTURE”, “AUTHENTIC TRADITIONS”, and “BREATHTAKING LANDSCAPES”.

At the bottom, create a dark horizontal food showcase strip with the centered heading “SAVOR THE TASTE OF GERMANY” in gold. Inside the strip, include exactly 5 framed food panels, each labeled in white uppercase beneath the image: “BREZEL”, “BRATWURST”, “KARTOFFELSALAT”, “SCHWARZWÄLDER KIRSCHTORTE”, and “GERMAN BEER”. Show each item as an appetizing close-up: a glossy pretzel on a plate, grilled sausages with sauerkraut, creamy potato salad, a slice of Black Forest cake topped with cream and cherry, and a frothy beer mug. Keep the overall design polished, high-end, poster-like, and suitable for a luxury destination campaign.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
创作一张复古风格的 【德国】 奢华旅行海报，设计感如同高端旅游宣传广告。采用宏大、对称的构图，搭配优雅的编辑排版，以及带有精致角落装饰细节的金色边框。在顶部中心，放置一枚小型金色国徽，下方为“DISCOVER”字样，随后是一个非常醒目的深海军蓝衬线标题 【GERMANY】。在其下方，添加金色大写标语“LAND OF HISTORY. CULTURE. INSPIRATION.”，随后是一行较小的经典衬线副标题“Timeless Traditions. Unforgettable Journeys.”，并配以黑、红、金三色的小型水平装饰线。

展示一幅壮丽的德国标志性风景全景蒙太奇，背景是黄金时刻下云朵稀疏、柔和发光的蓝天。左侧呈现一座如同新天鹅堡般的童话白色山顶城堡，坐落在秋色树木之上。远景中心放置雄伟的阿尔卑斯山脉。右侧展示一座历史悠久的欧洲河畔城市，密集的红顶房屋、横跨水面的拱形石桥、圆顶大教堂以及带有双尖塔的哥特式大教堂。添加平静的河面反射，右下角有一艘白色观光船。画面应充满怀旧、浪漫和令人向往的氛围，具备温暖的阳光、清晰的建筑细节和丰富的电影级色彩分级。

在下方中部，均匀水平排列 5 个金色线条艺术风格的文化图标，下方附带标签：“ICONIC LANDMARKS”（标志性地标）、“RICH HERITAGE”（深厚底蕴）、“VIBRANT CULTURE”（活力文化）、“AUTHENTIC TRADITIONS”（地道传统）和“BREATHTAKING LANDSCAPES”（绝美风景）。

底部创建一个深色水平美食展示条，居中标题为金色的“SAVOR THE TASTE OF GERMANY”。在展示条内，包含 5 个带边框的美食面板，每张图片下方均标注白色大写文字：“BREZEL”（德国碱水面包）、“BRATWURST”（德国烤肠）、“KARTOFFELSALAT”（德国土豆沙拉）、“SCHWARZWÄLDER KIRSCHTORTE”（黑森林蛋糕）和“GERMAN BEER”（德国啤酒）。每种食物均以诱人的特写形式呈现：盘中光泽诱人的碱水面包、配有酸菜的烤肠、奶油土豆沙拉、点缀着奶油和樱桃的黑森林蛋糕切片，以及泡沫丰富的啤酒杯。整体设计需保持精致、高端、海报感，适合用于奢华目的地宣传活动。
