# 正文 prompt
Using the provided reference image as the brand inspiration, transform the fried chicken burger and waffle fries meal into a polished one-page logo guidelines board for a restaurant brand named 【Can Dende】. Create a cheerful, modern fast-casual identity derived from the food in the reference: turn the crispy fried topping into a playful mascot/icon above the wordmark, and build a bold custom logo in a warm palette inspired by the photo. Design the output as a clean editorial brand sheet on an off-white background with navy and red typography, yellow scalloped borders at the top and bottom, and small sparkle doodles. Include these exact sections and counts: 1 main header reading "LOGO GUIDELINES" with the brand name beneath it; 1 "PRIMARY LOGO" section with the main logo and an "ABOUT THE LOGO" text block; 1 "LOGO VARIANTS" section with 3 variants labeled "FULL COLOR", "SINGLE COLOR", and "REVERSED"; 1 "COLOR VARIATIONS" section with 4 tiles labeled "YELLOW BACKGROUND", "NAVY BACKGROUND", "PINK BACKGROUND", and "NAVY ON CREAM"; 1 "MINIMUM SIZE" section showing the logo with a width guide and the text "1.25 in / 120 px MINIMUM WIDTH"; 1 "CLEAR SPACE" section with a spacing diagram based on the height of the letter C; 1 "INCORRECT USAGE" section with 4 wrong examples labeled "Don't change the colors", "Don't stretch or distort", "Don't rotate the logo", and "Don't add effects or shadows"; 1 "USAGE NOTES" section with 4 bullet points; and a handwritten-style closing tagline at bottom right that says 【It's a vibe. It's a smile. It's yours.】. Keep the composition crisp, print-ready, highly legible, and presentation-quality, like a real branding deliverable for a restaurant.

以提供的参考图片作为品牌灵感，将炸鸡汉堡和华夫薯条餐点转化为一份精美的单页 Logo 指南项目，用于名为 【Can Dende】 的餐厅品牌。根据参考图片中的食物，打造一种欢快、现代的快休闲品牌形象：将酥脆的炸鸡配料转化为文字标识上方俏皮的吉祥物/图标，并以照片中的暖色调为灵感，构建一个醒目的定制 Logo。将输出设计为一张简洁的编辑风格品牌页，背景为米白色，搭配海军蓝和红色字体，顶部和底部带有黄色扇形边框，并点缀少量闪光涂鸦。请包含以下确切部分及数量：1 个主标题，内容为“LOGO GUIDELINES”，下方标注品牌名称；1 个“PRIMARY LOGO”部分，包含主 Logo 和一段“ABOUT THE LOGO”文本块；1 个“LOGO VARIANTS”部分，包含 3 个变体，分别标注为“FULL COLOR”、“SINGLE COLOR”和“REVERSED”；1 个“COLOR VARIATIONS”部分，包含 4 个色块，分别标注为“YELLOW BACKGROUND”、“NAVY BACKGROUND”、“PINK BACKGROUND”和“NAVY ON CREAM”；1 个“MINIMUM SIZE”部分，展示带有宽度引导线的 Logo，并附带文本“1.25 in / 120 px MINIMUM WIDTH”；1 个“CLEAR SPACE”部分，包含基于字母 C 高度的间距示意图；1 个“INCORRECT USAGE”部分，包含 4 个错误示例，分别标注为“Don't change the colors”、“Don't stretch or distort”、“Don't rotate the logo”和“Don't add effects or shadows”；1 个“USAGE NOTES”部分，包含 4 个要点；以及右下角手写风格的结束语，内容为 【It's a vibe. It's a smile. It's yours.】。保持构图清晰、适合打印、高度易读且具备演示质量，如同真实的餐厅品牌交付成果。
