# 正文 prompt
Create a minimalist vertical art poster featuring a single man in clean side profile facing left, shown from about mid-thigh upward, wearing a tailored dark suit jacket, crisp white shirt, and a vintage 1940s-style black fedora with a ribbon band. His pose is upright, composed, and confident, evoking a classic noir protagonist. Render the figure in a refined monochrome photographic style with crisp editorial detail, rich tonal contrast, and dramatic Rembrandt-inspired lighting that sculpts the cheek, ear, jawline, hat brim, and shoulder with deep shadow. Place 1 bold perfectly round red disc directly behind the head and upper torso, centered like a halo or rising sun, creating a stark graphic focal point. The background should be a clean off-white or cream paper-textured surface with subtle grain and generous negative space around the subject. The lower half of the figure should dissolve into elongated black ink drips: count 12 distinct major dripping trails of varying thickness, hanging from the jacket hem and lower body, some long and straight, some tapering, with a few small splatters, as if the body is bleeding into the canvas. Keep the color palette strictly limited to black, red, and warm off-white. The overall mood should be moody, contemplative, elegant, slightly ominous, and modernist, like a high-end gallery poster blending film noir portraiture with surreal ink-paint abstraction.

创作一张极简主义竖版艺术海报，主体为一名身着干练西装的男子侧影，面向左侧，画面呈现从大腿中部向上的视角。他穿着剪裁得体的深色西装外套、挺括的白衬衫，并戴着一顶带有丝带装饰的 1940 年代复古黑色软呢帽。他的姿态挺拔、沉稳且自信，散发出经典的黑色电影主角气质。以精致的单色摄影风格渲染人物，细节清晰，色调对比强烈，并运用受伦勃朗启发的戏剧性光影，勾勒出脸颊、耳朵、下颌线、帽檐和肩部的深邃阴影。在头部和上半身的正后方放置一个醒目的正圆形红色圆盘，如同光环或初升的太阳般居中，形成强烈的视觉焦点。背景应为干净的灰白色或米色纸质纹理，带有细腻的颗粒感，并在主体周围保留充足的留白。人物下半身应溶解为细长的黑色墨滴：从西装下摆和身体下部垂下 12 条粗细不一的明显滴痕，有的长而直，有的逐渐变细，并伴有少量细小溅点，仿佛身体正在融入画布。配色方案严格限制在黑色、红色和温暖的灰白色。整体氛围应沉郁、深思、优雅、略带不祥且具有现代感，宛如一张将黑色电影肖像与超现实水墨抽象艺术相结合的高端画廊海报。
