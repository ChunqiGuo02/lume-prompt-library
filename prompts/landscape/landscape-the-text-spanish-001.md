# 正文 prompt
A satirical Spanish sports newspaper front page designed like a tabloid cover, full-frame and perfectly frontal, on slightly aged off-white newsprint with subtle paper texture, faint stains, and narrow black border lines. The masthead is a huge red SPORT logo across the top on a bright yellow banner. In the upper right, include 1 small interview promo box with a headshot of a footballer and the text block in Spanish: “ENTREVISTA CUBARSÍ ‘ESTOY PARA COSAS GRANDES’”. Under the masthead, add a yellow strip with bold black text “EXCLUSIVA MUNDIAL” and a small red lightning bolt. The main headline dominates the page in gigantic black condensed uppercase letters over 2 lines: “LAMILE FICHA POR LA PEÑA DEL BINGO”. Beneath it, add the subheadline in italic black Spanish text: “El crack pide dorsal el 10 y bocadillo de calamares en el contrato”. The main photo occupies the left-center and lower-middle of the page: a young footballer seen mostly from the back wearing a Barcelona home jersey in blue and garnet stripes, with the name “LAMILE” and the number “10” in large yellow lettering, standing in a celebratory stadium scene with confetti, bright lights, blurred fans, and people around him. He is holding or presenting a large bingo card board with the word “BINGO” at the top and “BINGO!” at the bottom. On the right side, create a vertical sidebar with 4 stacked content blocks: first, a photo of a calamari sandwich on a plate with the caption “El motivo del fichaje”; second, a red info box listing 3 humorous transfer facts in yellow and white text, exactly “EDAD: muy joven”, “FICHAJE: 4 cartones”, and “CLÁUSULA: una partida de parchís”; third, a quotes column with 3 stacked mini-items, each with a small portrait and Spanish caption: “XAVI: ‘No me lo esperaba, pero respeto’”, “LAPORTA: ‘Renegociaremos en el próximo bingo’”, and “EL VESTUARIO: Le preparan línea y bingo de bienvenida”; fourth, a small motorsport teaser at the bottom with a driver image and the text “FÓRMULA 1 ALONSO SUEÑA CON EL PODIO EN YEDA”. Along the bottom strip, include 3 small teaser panels: 1 woman footballer holding a trophy with the text “CHAMPIONS FEMENINA LA REINA AITANA QUIERE OTRA CORONA”, 1 basketball panel with two Barça players and the text “BÁSQUET EL BARÇA, A UN PAS DE LA FINAL FOUR”, and the Formula 1 teaser panel already integrated at the right edge. Add tiny newspaper metadata below the masthead on the left in Spanish, including issue number, date, and price, such as “Nº 16.743 MIÉRCOLES 22 DE ABRIL DE 2026 2,00 EUROS”. The overall style should be hyper-realistic printed newspaper cover photography and editorial layout, with authentic magazine typography, strong black headlines, saturated sports-photo colors, comedic transfer-rumor parody tone, and seamless believable print design.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
一张设计成小报封面的讽刺风格西班牙体育报纸头版，全画幅且完全正面视角，印在略显陈旧的米白色新闻纸上，带有细腻的纸张纹理、淡淡的污渍和细窄的黑色边框。报头顶部横跨一条亮黄色横幅，印有巨大的红色“SPORT”标志。右上角包含 1 个小型采访推广框，内含一名足球运动员的头像，以及西班牙语文本块：“ENTREVISTA CUBARSÍ ‘ESTOY PARA COSAS GRANDES’”。在报头下方，添加一条带有粗体黑色文字“EXCLUSIVA MUNDIAL”的黄色长条，并配有一个红色小闪电图标。主标题以巨大的黑色压缩大写字母占据页面核心位置，分为 2 行：“LAMILE FICHA POR LA PEÑA DEL BINGO”。在其下方，添加斜体黑色西班牙语副标题：“El crack pide dorsal el 10 y bocadillo de calamares en el contrato”。主照片占据页面左中及中下部：一名年轻足球运动员背影为主，身穿蓝红条纹的巴塞罗那主场球衣，球衣上印有大号黄色字母“LAMILE”和数字“10”，站在充满纸屑、强光、模糊球迷及人群的庆祝球场场景中。他手持或展示着一张巨大的宾果游戏项目，顶部写有“BINGO”，底部写有“BINGO!”。在右侧，创建一个包含 4 个堆叠内容块的垂直侧边栏：第一部分是一张盘中鱿鱼三明治的照片，配文“El motivo del fichaje”；第二部分是一个红色信息框，列出 3 条幽默的转会事实，文字为黄色和白色，内容为：“EDAD: muy joven”、“FICHAJE: 4 cartones”以及“CLÁUSULA: una partida de parchís”；第三部分是一个引语栏，包含 3 个堆叠的小项目，每个项目都有一个小肖像和西班牙语说明：“XAVI: ‘No me lo esperaba, pero respeto’”、“LAPORTA: ‘Renegociaremos en el próximo bingo’”以及“EL VESTUARIO: Le preparan línea y bingo de bienvenida”；第四部分是底部的一个小型赛车预告，配有车手图像和文字“FÓRMULA 1 ALONSO SUEÑA CON EL PODIO EN YEDA”。沿底部长条，包含 3 个小型预告面板：1 名手持奖杯的女足运动员，配文“CHAMPIONS FEMENINA LA REINA AITANA QUIERE OTRA CORONA”；1 个篮球面板，包含两名巴萨球员，配文“BÁSQUET EL BARÇA, A UN PAS DE LA FINAL FOUR”；以及已整合在右边缘的 F1 赛车预告面板。在报头左下方添加微小的西班牙语报纸元数据，包括期号、日期和价格，例如“Nº 16.743 MIÉRCOLES 22 DE ABRIL DE 2026 2,00 EUROS”。整体风格应为超写实的印刷报纸封面摄影和编辑排版，具备地道的杂志排版、强烈的黑色标题、饱和的体育照片色彩、喜剧性的转会传闻恶搞基调，以及天衣无缝且可信的印刷设计。
