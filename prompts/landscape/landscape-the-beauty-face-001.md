# 正文 prompt
Luxury natural skincare ad campaign shot in a sun-dappled forest clearing, styled like a premium editorial beauty poster for 【WILLOW & ROOT】. Center a beautiful young woman with sun-kissed skin and loose blonde hair in a thick side braid, wearing a soft beige spaghetti-strap slip dress, seated behind a rustic moss-covered log table, holding delicate white wildflowers near her face. Her face is obscured by a clean rectangular block in a warm tan tone, creating an anonymous model effect. In the foreground on the log, arrange exactly 5 beauty products in a polished e-commerce lineup from left to right: a green glass dropper bottle labeled organic face oil with bamboo cap, a round cream blush compact with dusty rose lid, a tinted lip balm stick in muted terracotta packaging, a frosted pale-green pump bottle labeled dewy serum with bamboo pump, and a closed bamboo compact engraved with the brand name. Add soft white wildflowers and lush green foliage around the products and edges of the frame. Use warm golden-hour backlighting, creamy bokeh, shallow depth of field, soft lens glow, natural green and beige palette, high-end botanical wellness aesthetic, photorealistic skin and packaging, centered composition, vertical 4:5 layout. Overlay elegant dark serif typography at the top with the headline 【Rooted in Radiance】 on two lines, a smaller sans-serif tagline beneath reading 【Nature-Powered. Skin-Loving.】, separated by a small leaf icon and thin horizontal divider lines. Add 2 circular promotional badges: on the upper left, an olive-green badge with a leaf icon and the text 【PLANT-BASED + CRUELTY FREE】; on the upper right, a beige badge with the text 【15% OFF FIRST ORDER】. Place a small white Pollo.ai watermark in the top-right corner. Premium beauty advertising, clean luxury composition, modern mobile-first e-commerce campaign.

在阳光斑驳的森林空地拍摄的奢华天然护肤品广告，风格类似于 【WILLOW & ROOT】 的高端美妆海报。画面中央是一位美丽的年轻女性，她拥有被阳光亲吻过的肌肤和松散的金色侧编发，身穿柔软的米色吊带裙，坐在铺满苔藓的乡村原木桌后，手持精致的白色野花靠近脸部。她的脸部被一个暖棕色的简洁矩形色块遮挡，营造出匿名模特的艺术效果。在前景的原木上，从左至右整齐排列 5 款精致的电商产品：一瓶标有有机面部精油的绿色玻璃滴管瓶（配竹制盖）、一个带有灰粉色盖子的圆形腮红膏、一支哑光陶土色包装的润色唇膏、一瓶标有水润精华的磨砂浅绿色泵头瓶（配竹制泵头），以及一个刻有品牌名称的封闭式竹制粉饼盒。在产品周围和画面边缘点缀柔和的白色野花和茂密的绿色植物。采用温暖的黄金时刻背光、奶油般虚化的背景、浅景深、柔和的镜头光晕、自然的绿色与米色调、高端植物健康美学、照片级真实的皮肤与包装质感、居中构图、竖版 4:5 布局。在顶部叠加优雅的深色衬线字体，显示两行标题 【Rooted in Radiance】，下方配以较小的无衬线标语 【Nature-Powered. Skin-Loving.】，中间由一个小叶子图标和细水平分割线隔开。添加 2 个圆形促销徽章：左上角为一个带有叶子图标和 【PLANT-BASED + CRUELTY FREE】 文字的橄榄绿徽章；右上角为一个带有 【15% OFF FIRST ORDER】 文字的米色徽章。在右上角放置一个小型的白色 Pollo.ai 水印。高端美妆广告，简洁奢华构图，现代移动端优先的电商营销活动。
