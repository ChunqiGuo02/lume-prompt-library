# 正文 prompt
Create a dramatic anime sci-fi poster of 【Puru Two】, inspired by a cyber-newtype pilot from a mecha war setting. The composition is a vertically oriented character key visual with one central full-body female figure in a sleek red and black pilot bodysuit, shown standing confidently in a slightly low-angle pose. She has short layered orange hair with long side bangs, pale skin, and a serious, emotionally distant expression. Her suit is glossy crimson with black side panels, a high collar, fitted seams, a front zipper line, dark gloves, a utility belt, and military insignia on the shoulders and chest. The background is a dark, space-like abstract field with black, crimson, magenta, and scattered star-like textures, splattered energy effects, and a gritty futuristic propaganda-poster mood. Surround the central figure with 3 inset panels: a top-right close-up of her eyes and upper face reflected through a transparent cockpit or HUD glass with red interface lights and small technical readouts, a mid-right soft pale portrait of a ghostly young girl in a loose white gown against a luminous misty white-gray background, and a bottom-right close-up of 1 gloved hand gripping a mechanical control lever with illuminated cockpit instrumentation. Add dense graphic design elements and typography throughout the left and right edges, combining elegant serif English text, Japanese vertical title text, compact technical labels, and mecha specification blocks. Include visible text such as the large title "PURU TWO", "NEO ZEON", "CYBER-NEWTYPE", "MOBILE SUIT GUNDAM ZZ", "PSYCHO GUNDAM MK-II", percentage/status readouts like "99.8%", and other small control-panel annotations. Use a high-detail painted anime illustration style, cinematic red lighting, glossy fabric highlights, sharp linework, subtle screen reflections, layered UI overlays, and a premium poster layout reminiscent of a luxury sci-fi character dossier.
