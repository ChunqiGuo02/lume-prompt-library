# 正文 prompt
A bold retro propaganda-style poster illustration with a limited palette of red, black, and warm cream on a lightly aged paper texture. The composition features a dynamic anime-inspired action scene in the center: a martial artist in a white gi with a black belt carries a long-haired woman over one shoulder in a fireman's carry or wrestling throw pose. The fighter is shown from the knees up in a powerful wide stance, facing left, with the woman draped horizontally across the shoulders, her bare legs lifted and bent, her long dark hair flowing downward, and a black wide-brim cowboy hat still on her head. Render both figures in high-contrast inked line art with thick black shadows and sharp graphic contours, mixing manga character design with vintage screen-printed poster energy. Behind them, fill the background with aggressive geometric rays and wedges: 6 major red and black angular shapes radiating outward across the cream backdrop, creating a sense of impact and motion. In the top right corner, add 1 circular emblem containing a fierce gorilla or ape head icon with red accents. Across the lower third, place large bold condensed block typography integrated into the composition: the smaller line reads “SCOTTISH RECIPE:” and the huge main title reads “MINCE AND TATTIES”. Make the text red on cream and cream on black in layered diagonal banner panels, with the words occupying 3 text groupings total: “SCOTTISH RECIPE:”, “MINCE AND”, and “TATTIES”. Emphasize dramatic diagonal layout, posterized shadows, distressed print texture, and the feeling of a vintage Soviet constructivist or pulp exploitation food poster fused with martial arts manga aesthetics.
