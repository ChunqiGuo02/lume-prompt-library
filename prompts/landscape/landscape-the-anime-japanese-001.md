# 正文 prompt
Create an emotional Japanese movie trailer banner poster in a tall vertical composition, cinematic anime illustration style, set on a rainy nighttime city street in Japan filled with wet asphalt reflections, neon shop signs, and softly blurred high-rise buildings receding into the distance. In the center foreground, show 1 young anime girl in side profile facing left, standing alone under 1 transparent plastic umbrella with visible raindrops. She has long dark purple hair tied in a high ponytail, a slightly melancholic expression, and wears an oversized teal hoodie layered under a loose pink-and-teal plaid jacket. The mood is lonely, wistful, and dramatic, like a heartfelt live-action Japanese film advertisement reimagined as anime key art. Use cool blue and cyan tones with pink neon accents, glossy rain reflections, shallow depth of field, soft atmospheric haze, and subtle rim lighting from street signs. Add large handwritten white Japanese title text at the top reading 【AIの涙】, with 1 smaller white subtitle line beneath it reading 【彼女は、なぜ泣いたのか。】. At the bottom, add 1 bold white release line reading 【2024年秋 全国ロードショー】. Keep the character as the emotional focal point, framed mid-shot from about waist up, umbrella arcing over her head, with the empty rain-soaked street behind her emphasizing isolation and cinematic sadness.
