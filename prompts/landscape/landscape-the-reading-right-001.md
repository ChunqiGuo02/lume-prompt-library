A photorealistic Japanese izakaya advertisement poster in a warm, cinematic night-time mood, designed like a street-front restaurant flyer. The composition is vertical and densely layered. Her pose is relaxed and inviting, looking toward the camera, with a soft nightlife ambiance. Along the far right edge, add vertical white Japanese text reading 今日も一日、おかれさまです。 Near the lower right over the woman’s torso, place a red circular stamp-like badge with white handwritten text arranged diagonally and vertically: 旨い！安い！居心地◎. Across the bottom foreground, show exactly 4 plated menu items lined up side by side on rustic ceramic dishes: 1) two glazed grilled chicken skewers with char marks, labeled 焼き鳥 盛り合わせ and priced 580円; 2) a sashimi assortment with slices of tuna, salmon, white fish, shiso leaves and wasabi, labeled お刺身 盛り合わせ and priced 780円; 3) a plate of fried chicken karaage pieces with a lemon wedge, labeled 若鶏の唐揚げ and priced 480円; 4) sliced rolled dashi omelet topped with chopped green onion and served with grated daikon, labeled 出汁巻き玉子 and priced 380円. At the bottom left, include a glowing red paper lantern graphic with black vertical text 居酒屋. Across the bottom center on a dark band, add large bold white brush lettering reading でかい！うまい！安い！ and below it smaller white text reading みんなの憩いの場、蓮華食堂へようこそ！ Add a decorative red lotus-like emblem near the top sign and another matching emblem in the bottom right corner. Use rich amber highlights, strong contrast, glossy food styling, realistic skin and drink textures, commercial poster polish, and a nostalgic Showa-inspired izakaya promotion aesthetic.
