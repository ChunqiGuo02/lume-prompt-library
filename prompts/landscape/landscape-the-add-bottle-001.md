# 正文 prompt
Create a clean luxury skincare product advertisement in a square 1:1 layout with a warm beige studio background and strong natural sunlight casting soft palm-leaf shadows across the wall. Place 1 amber glass dropper bottle on the left-center, standing upright on a round cream stone pedestal. The bottle has a glossy gold collar and a matte white rubber dropper top. Add a white rectangular label with elegant minimal typography: brand name "【NATURELLE SKINCARE】" at the top, a large orange product name "【VITAMIN C】", then black text reading "BRIGHTENING SERUM" and smaller subtext "With Hyaluronic Acid & Niacinamide". Surround the pedestal with 3 visible glossy green tropical leaves, arranged from the left edge and behind the bottle for a fresh botanical look. On the right side, place a large bold black serif headline in 3 stacked lines reading "【Glow Naturally. Every Day.】". Beneath it, add a smaller clean sans-serif benefits line reading "【Brightens | Hydrates | Protects】". Below that, include 3 circular black outline ingredient or values icons in a horizontal row, each with a simple leaf-style symbol inside; add tiny caption text beneath them with the same slightly imperfect ad-like wording seen in the original, including "Barban Free", "Dermicologyg", and "Creuty-free". At the lower right, place a glossy rounded green call-to-action button with white bold text "【Shop Now】". Add 1 sliced orange on the far right edge, partially cropped, with vivid juicy texture to reinforce the vitamin C theme. Include a small white "Pollo.ai" watermark in the top-right corner. Style the whole image as a polished commercial product mockup, photorealistic, high-end beauty branding, soft warm highlights, subtle shadows, balanced composition, sharp label detail, premium ecommerce social ad aesthetic.

Every Day.】"。在标题下方，添加一行较小的简洁无衬线功效说明，内容为 "【Brightens | Hydrates | Protects】"。下方横向排列 3 个圆形黑色轮廓的成分或价值图标，每个图标内含一个简单的叶子符号；在图标下方添加微小的说明文字，采用与原版广告中略显不完美的风格，包括 "Barban Free"、"Dermicologyg" 和 "Creuty-free"。在右下角放置一个光泽的圆形绿色行动呼吁按钮，上面印有白色粗体文字 "【Shop Now】"。在最右侧边缘放置 1 片切开的橙子，部分被裁剪，呈现出生动多汁的质感，以强化维生素 C 的主题。在右上角添加一个小的白色 "Pollo.ai" 水印。将整张图片设计为精致的商业产品样机，照片级真实感，高端美妆品牌风格，柔和温暖的高光，微妙的阴影，平衡的构图，清晰的标签细节，优质的电商社交广告美学。
