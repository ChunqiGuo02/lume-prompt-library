# 正文 prompt
Create a seamless-looking 360-degree equirectangular HDRI environment map of a futuristic urban expressway at night, viewed from the middle of a wide wet roadway beneath elevated highways in a dense modern city. Use a panoramic full-surround composition with strong fisheye-like equirectangular distortion at the top and bottom edges, designed for use as a Blender world background. The scene should feature 2 large curving elevated highway ramps, one on the left and one on the right, arching inward toward the center, with multiple supporting pillars and additional overpass layers receding into the distance. Surround the roadway with a skyline of illuminated high-rise office towers and apartment buildings, with cool blue and cyan windows and a few magenta and purple accent lights. Make the road surface glossy and rain-soaked, reflecting the city lights in long horizontal streaks of neon blue, violet, and pink. Add subtle light trails and luminous reflections across the pavement, creating a cyberpunk mood. The sky should be dark, cloudy, and atmospheric with a faint bright glow at the zenith and soft haze around distant buildings. Keep the overall palette dominated by deep indigo, electric blue, cyan, and purple. The image should feel cinematic, moody, ultra-detailed, photorealistic, high dynamic range, with realistic urban lighting and mirrored wet-surface reflections, suitable as an HDRI backdrop. Include slight panorama seam artifacts and stretched distortion near the poles so it feels like an AI-generated but usable environment map.

创建一张无缝的 360 度等距柱状投影 HDRI 环境贴图，展现未来主义城市夜间高速公路的景象，视角位于密集现代城市高架桥下宽阔湿润的道路中央。采用全景环绕构图，顶部和底部边缘带有强烈的鱼眼式等距柱状畸变，专为 Blender 世界背景设计。场景应包含 2 条大型弯曲高架匝道，左右各一，向中心拱起，并有多个支撑柱和向远处延伸的多层立交桥。道路四周环绕着灯火通明的写字楼和公寓楼天际线，窗户呈现冷蓝色和青色，并点缀少量洋红色和紫色装饰灯。路面应具有光泽且被雨水浸湿，反射出长条状的霓虹蓝、紫和粉色城市灯光。在路面上添加微妙的光轨和发光反射，营造出赛博朋克氛围。天空应为深沉、多云且富有质感的夜空，天顶处有微弱亮光，远处的建筑物周围环绕着柔和的雾气。整体色调以深靛蓝、电光蓝、青色和紫色为主。画面应具有电影感、情绪化、超高细节、照片级真实感，并具备逼真的城市照明和湿润表面的镜像反射，适合作为 HDRI 背景使用。包含轻微的全景接缝伪影和极点附近的拉伸畸变，使其呈现出 AI 生成但可用的环境贴图质感。
