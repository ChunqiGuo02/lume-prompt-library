# 正文 prompt
Use case: historical-scene. Create an atmospheric historical documentary scene in the Roman Forum during a sudden spring rainstorm, around the 1st century CE. Senators lift hems of wool garments, merchants cover baskets, a child runs under a colonnade, and rainwater channels through worn stone streets. Emphasize motion, wet fabric, believable architecture, and everyday human reactions rather than a posed tableau. Style: cinematic realism with accurate materials, no fantasy elements, no modern objects. Lighting: storm clouds with a break of warm sun behind marble columns. No readable modern text, logos, or watermark.

使用场景：历史场景。在公元1世纪左右，罗马广场上营造一个充满氛围的历史纪录片场景，当时正值一场突如其来的春雨。参议员们撩起羊毛衣物的下摆，商人们用布盖住他们的货物，一个孩子从柱廊下跑过，雨水顺着破旧的石板街道流淌。重点展现人物的动作、湿润的织物、逼真的建筑风格以及人们日常的反应，而非刻意摆拍的画面。风格：电影般的写实主义，使用真实的材料，不包含任何奇幻元素或现代物品。灯光效果：乌云密布，但在大理石柱子后方偶尔透出温暖的阳光。画面中不应出现任何现代文字、标志或水印。
