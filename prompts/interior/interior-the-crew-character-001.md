# 正文 prompt

Replace the infographic/chart layout with a clean 2x2 magazine spread on an off-white background. Add a large serif brand header reading 【J.CREW】 and an italic subhead reading 【Gritty’s Off Hours: Cool & Casual】. Show exactly 4 styled outfit photos of the same character, keeping the fuzzy orange fur, big eyes, and black helmet, but placing the mascot in modern menswear inspired by the “best” colors from the reference: 1) “CLASSIC CREW” — seated on a stool in a navy sweater over a crisp white oxford with dark denim; 2) “EASY LAYERS” — standing casually in a teal overshirt, white tee, and charcoal trousers; 3) “POP OF PLUM” — relaxed pose in a plum cardigan with dark pants; 4) “MODERN SPORT” — seated in a black sporty layer with black pants, white socks, and white sneakers. Give each panel a short catalog-style caption under it matching those 4 titles and concise fashion copy. Use soft natural indoor lighting, minimal home-interior editorial settings, premium catalog photography, and a tasteful upscale preppy aesthetic.
