# 正文 prompt
A polished commercial advertising photo inside a modern transit-station or public waiting area, shot straight-on with a slightly low eye level, featuring a large illuminated Pepsi vending machine on the left and center of the frame. A young man is seen from behind, wearing a light gray hoodie, black athletic shorts, white crew socks, and white sneakers, stretching upward to grab a Pepsi bottle from the very top shelf. He is standing precariously on a vertical stack of exactly 4 red Coca-Cola cans arranged one on top of another on the floor, making the visual joke that getting Pepsi is literally worth the extra steps. The vending machine is fully stocked and brightly lit with cool white interior lighting: the top shelf contains multiple Pepsi bottles, the middle shelves contain assorted clear, green, yellow, and amber bottled drinks, and the lower shelf displays rows of red Coca-Cola cans. The machine exterior is Pepsi blue with a large Pepsi globe logo and the word PEPSI on the left side panel, plus a payment/control panel on the right side of the machine. Across the top of the image, place a huge bold white all-caps sans-serif headline reading 【WORTH THE EXTRA STEPS.】 centered against a dark upper wall. At the bottom center, add small clean white sans-serif brand copy reading 【Choose Pepsi.】. On the right wall, include a framed Pepsi poster with bold stacked text reading “REFRESH RECHARGE REPEAT” above a Pepsi logo. Add a simple metal bench beneath the poster. Use glossy floor reflections, crisp focus, premium ad photography, realistic proportions, high dynamic range, and a clean brand-safe composition with strong contrast between Pepsi blue, Coca-Cola red, and neutral gray surroundings.
