The 12 disciples should vary in age and appearance, with curly or wavy dark hair, beards, and earth-toned robes in cream, brown, tan, muted blue, and dusty gray, leaning inward in conversation and attention. The room should have rough plaster walls, dark exposed wooden ceiling beams, a heavy wooden door at the back, simple wall niches, and small oil lamps or candle sconces casting warm amber light. On the table, show rustic period-appropriate food and vessels: round flatbread, grapes, olives, shallow bowls, clay plates, ceramic cups, and copper or earthenware goblets, with one small candle near the center creating a focal glow. Composition should be wide and intimate, slightly crowded at the foreground edges, with several figures partially cropped to enhance realism and immersion. Lighting should be low, golden, and dramatic, with soft shadows, a reverent mood, realistic skin tones, natural fabric folds, and rich cinematic contrast. Render in a photorealistic style with ultra-detailed textures, subtle depth of field, and the emotional tone of a historical biblical film still.
