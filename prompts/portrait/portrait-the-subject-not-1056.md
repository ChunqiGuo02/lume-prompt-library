# 正文 prompt
TASK: Use the subject from the uploaded photo as the exact person in the final image.  SUBJECT RULES (STRICT): - Use the uploaded photo as the sole reference for the person. - Preserve the subject’s identity, facial features, body proportions, hairstyle, clothing, and overall likeness. - Do NOT change the subject’s face, age, gender, or identity. - Do NOT stylize the subject into a cartoon or illustration. - The subject must remain fully photorealistic.  SCENE & COMPOSITION: Create a surreal, cinematic photograph of the subject suspended in mid-air, floating as if gently falling, frozen in perfect stillness high above the Earth. The subject’s body appears weightless with a natural falling posture. Clothing and loose elements subtly ripple in the wind.  ENVIRONMENT: - High-altitude sky scene above the cloud layer. - Overcast yet luminous sky with towering storm clouds breaking apart. - Soft, realistic sunlight rays piercing through gaps in the clouds. - Natural volumetric light beams and atmospheric depth. - Subtle cloud vapor and mist drifting around the subject. - A distant commercial passenger airplane appears in the background, small in scale, flying horizontally through the clouds. - The airplane must remain far behind the subject and never overlap or distract from them. - A few birds are also present at varying distances, closer than the airplane, with slight motion blur to reinforce depth and scale.  LIGHTING & COLOR: - Ultra-realistic natural lighting. - Cool blues and soft whites dominate the palette. - Warm golden highlights from sunlight striking cloud edges and parts of the subject. - High dynamic range with realistic contrast.  CAMERA & REALISM: - Cinematic photography style. - Realistic depth of field (no artificial or extreme blur). - Correct perspective and scale consistent with high altitude aviation. - No fantasy lighting, no glowing outlines, no surreal distortions beyond the floating effect.  MOOD & STYLE: - Dramatic yet calm. - Minimalist composition. - Strong sense of altitude, silence, and suspension. - Hyper-realistic weather, clouds, and atmospheric detail. - Looks like a high-end cinematic still, not digital art.  HARD RULES: - Do NOT change the subject’s identity or pose beyond floating. - Do NOT add text, logos, UI, or watermarks. - Do NOT introduce sci-fi or fantasy elements. - Keep everything physically believable and grounded in realism.
