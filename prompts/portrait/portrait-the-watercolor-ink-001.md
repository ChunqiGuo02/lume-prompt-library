# 正文 prompt
"Create a high-detail watercolor and ink portrait of the exact same face (100% match). Preserve the facial features precisely. The artwork should blend soft flowing watercolor washes in pastel blues and warm pinks with expressive black ink linework defining contours and hair strands. The colors should bleed naturally into each other with visible paper texture and delicate brush splatter. Use light transparency layers to convey emotion, depth, and a sense of calm movement. Place the subject in front of a textured wall (e.g., brick, stucco, or painted surface) instead of a plain background, keeping the wall subtle so it complements the portrait without distracting. Maintain a soft, artistic feel with the wall integrated naturally behind the figure."

## 中文译文（源站提供）
创作一幅高度精细的水彩和水墨肖像画，描绘同一张脸（100% 匹配）。精确保留面部特征。作品应将柔和流淌的粉蓝色和暖粉色水彩晕染与富有表现力的黑色水墨线条相结合，勾勒出轮廓和发丝。色彩应自然地相互渗透，并展现可见的纸张纹理和细腻的笔触飞溅。使用轻薄的透明图层来传达情感、深度和一种平静的动感。将主体置于纹理墙（例如砖墙、灰泥墙或涂漆表面）前，而非纯色背景，同时保持墙壁的微妙感，使其与肖像相得益彰而不分散注意力。保持柔和的艺术感，让墙壁自然地融入人物身后。
