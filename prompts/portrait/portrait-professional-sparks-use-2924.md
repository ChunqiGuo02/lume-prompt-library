# 正文 prompt
Use case: photorealistic-natural. Create a professional documentary photo of workers repairing a large steel ship hull at dusk. Welding sparks arc downward while one worker steadies a cable, another checks a clipboard, and a crane moves slowly in the background. Emphasize realistic scale, safety gear, layered industrial depth, smoke, reflections on wet concrete, and believable teamwork. Camera: 50mm professional photography, slightly low angle, natural motion in sparks and hands. Avoid logos, readable company names, heroic posing, and watermark.
