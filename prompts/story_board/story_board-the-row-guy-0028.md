# 正文 prompt
A 3x3 grid collage of 9 distinct, awkwardly posed vintage 1990s American yearbook portraits.

**Layout & Subjects:**

* **Center Panel (Row 2, Column 2):** Contains the main subject: [use the person in the attached photo as a late teenager], with a deer-in-headlights look wearing a very loud, geometrically patterned "ugly" knit sweater in clashing colors (purple, green, orange, blue) layered over a white collared shirt.

* **Surrounding 8 Panels:** Fill with diverse classmates featuring comically awkward expressions and exaggerated 90s styles:
    * **Top Row:** A girl with a massive, frizzy perm and thick glasses grinning goofily; a guy with a huge, feathered mullet in a denim jacket looking uncomfortable; a girl with a severe bowl cut and a strained, toothy smile.
    * **Middle Row (Left/Right):** A guy with a tall flat-top haircut giving a cheesy thumbs-up; another guy with a severe center part and a confused, open-mouthed stare.
      * **Bottom Row:** A girl with wildly crimped hair and a pained grimace; a guy wearing large, thick-rimmed tortoise-shell glasses, textured pastel knit crewneck sweater layered over a white collared shirt has a polite, slightly awkward smile; another girl with huge, teased bangs and a forced, tight-lipped grin.
**Style & Tech:**
Classic "Olan Mills" studio aesthetic applied uniformly to all 9 panels. All backgrounds are the generic mottled blue hand-painted canvas. Lighting is flat, broad studio flash with subtle rim lighting. Image quality features soft focus, heavy film grain, warm Kodak Gold 200 color grading, and a soft vignette over each individual panel.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
一张 3x3 网格拼贴画，由 9 张截然不同、姿势笨拙的 1990 年代美国复古毕业照组成。

**布局与人物：**

*   **中心面板（第 2 行，第 2 列）：** 包含主要人物：[使用附图中青少年时期的人物]，他眼神呆滞，穿着一件颜色冲突（紫色、绿色、橙色、蓝色）的非常扎眼、几何图案的“丑陋”针织毛衣，内搭一件白色翻领衬衫。

*   **周围 8 个面板：** 填充着各种同学，他们表情滑稽笨拙，90 年代风格夸张：
    *   **顶行：** 一个女孩顶着一头蓬松的大卷发，戴着厚厚的眼镜，傻乎乎地笑着；一个留着巨大羽毛状鲻鱼头、穿着牛仔夹克的男生，看起来很不自在；一个留着齐耳短发、笑容僵硬的女孩。
    *   **中行（左/右）：** 一个留着高平头、竖着俗气大拇指的男生；另一个留着中分、表情困惑、张着嘴巴的男生。
    *   **底行：** 一个头发狂野卷曲、表情痛苦的女孩；一个戴着大而厚的玳瑁框眼镜、穿着纹理柔和的淡彩色圆领针织衫内搭白色翻领衬衫的男生，带着礼貌而略显尴尬的微笑；另一个留着巨大蓬松刘海、强颜欢笑的女孩。

**风格与技术：**
经典的“Olan Mills”影楼美学统一应用于所有 9 个面板。所有背景都是通用的斑驳蓝色手绘画布。灯光是平坦、宽泛的影室闪光灯，带有微妙的轮廓光。图像质量特点是柔焦、厚重的胶片颗粒、温暖的柯达金 200 色调，以及每个独立面板上的柔和晕影。
