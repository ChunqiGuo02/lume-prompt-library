# 正文 prompt
**Prompt Title:** The Austrian Oak's Volumetric Anatomy

**Prompt:**

A monumental, highly graphic, high-contrast silhouette of Arnold Schwarzenegger in his 1970s prime, perfectly executing a classic Side Chest pose. The exterior outline of the physique is rendered as a razor-sharp, pure black (0% light) shape, creating an extreme visual boundary.

The entire internal negative space within the silhouette is intricately filled with a photorealistic, dense Austrian mountain forest landscape—specifically, deep coniferous pines and firs of the Hohe Tauern region. The scene within the body is captured at the peak of Golden Hour sunset. Deep, rich hues of fire orange, crimson, and violet dominate the internal sky.

Crucially, intense, focused volumetric god rays and sun shafts cut dramatically through the thick forest canopy, filtering dust and mist. These light shafts meticulously trace and illuminate the underlying anatomy: the beams define the striations of the pectoral muscle, the peaks of the biceps, and the separation of the deltoids. The light filtering through the trees *is* the muscle definition.

**Camera Settings:**
Shot on Hasselblad X2D, 135mm prime lens. Wide aperture (f/2.8) for maximum environmental detail within the silhouette.

**Lighting Details:**
Extreme hard backlighting defines the silhouette against a neutral, slightly hazy background. Internal lighting is Deep Golden Hour, intense volumetric mist/fog, strong rim light defining individual tree branches and forming anatomical detail.

**Stylistic Notes:**
Masterpiece cinematic photography. Hyper-detailed realism within the silhouette. Maximal resolution. Aspect ratio 16:9. --ar 16:9 --v 6.0 --s 750

**提示：**

剪影内部的整个负空间被精细地填充了逼真的、茂密的奥地利山地森林景观——特别是霍赫陶恩（Hohe Tauern）地区的深色针叶松和冷杉。身体内部的场景捕捉了黄金时段日落的巅峰时刻。深邃、浓郁的火橙色、深红色和紫色主导着内部的天空。

至关重要的是，强烈、集中的体积光束和阳光穿透浓密的森林冠层，过滤着灰尘和薄雾。这些光束精确地勾勒并照亮了底层的解剖结构：光束界定了胸肌的纹理、二头肌的峰值以及三角肌的分离。透过树木过滤的光线*就是*肌肉的线条。
