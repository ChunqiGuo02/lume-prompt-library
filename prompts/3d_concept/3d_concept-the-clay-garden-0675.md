Place them near the existing rabbit as if all three are friends sharing the garden moment. The hog should have a soft pink clay body, tiny hooves, and a gentle smile. The squirrel should have a warm brown clay body, curled tail, and hold a tiny clay acorn. Match the stop-motion clay texture, fingerprints, scale, lighting, shadows, and pastel paper background.
