# 正文 prompt
A whimsical high-detail oil-painting parody of Michelangelo’s Creation of Adam, but all human figures are replaced by teddy bears. On the left, a single plush teddy bear sits reclined on a rocky slope, posed like Adam, with soft golden-brown curly fur, round ears, shiny black eyes, a small dark nose, and a slightly open mouth. Its left arm supports its body while its right arm stretches outward, index paw extended toward the other bear. On the right, a larger central teddy bear posed like God emerges from a deep reddish-burgundy swirling mantle, wearing a flowing white draped robe, leaning forward with one outstretched arm and pointed paw reaching toward the left bear, almost touching fingertips. Surrounding the robed bear inside the mantle are 8 additional smaller teddy bears clustered tightly together, peeking from different angles like attendant figures. The composition exactly echoes the iconic near-touching hands scene, with the gap between fingertips centered. Use a cracked fresco wall background in warm beige tones, muted Renaissance palette, soft directional light, realistic fabric folds, detailed plush texture, subtle shadows, and a museum-quality painted surface. Blend classical Renaissance fresco aesthetics with charming surreal humor, keeping the scene elegant rather than cartoonish.

一幅充满奇趣且细节丰富的油画风格《创世纪》戏仿作品，米开朗基罗原作中的人类形象全部被泰迪熊取代。画面左侧，一只毛绒泰迪熊斜倚在岩石坡上，姿态模仿亚当，拥有柔软的金棕色卷毛、圆耳朵、闪亮的黑眼睛、小巧的深色鼻子和微张的嘴。它的左臂支撑着身体，右臂向外伸展，食指爪尖伸向另一只熊。画面右侧，一只体型较大的泰迪熊模仿上帝的姿态，从深红酒色的漩涡状斗篷中显现，身披飘逸的白色垂褶长袍，身体前倾，伸出一只手臂，指尖爪尖向左侧的熊探去，几乎触碰到对方。在斗篷内，围绕着身穿长袍的泰迪熊，还有 8 只体型较小的泰迪熊紧密簇拥，从不同角度探出头来，宛如随从。构图精准还原了标志性的“指尖触碰”场景，两指间的空隙位于画面中心。背景采用暖米色的裂纹壁画质感，运用柔和的文艺复兴色调、柔和的定向光、逼真的织物褶皱、细腻的毛绒质感、微妙的阴影以及博物馆级的绘画表面。将古典文艺复兴壁画美学与迷人的超现实幽默感融为一体，保持画面优雅而不失童趣。
