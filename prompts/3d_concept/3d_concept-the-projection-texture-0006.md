Crucial: Projection Physics & Texture Mapping:
FULL FACADE COVERAGE: The projection mapping covers the entire visible facade of the station from edge to edge. No part of the red brick wall remains unlit; the whole building is transformed into a colorful amusement park canvas.

SURFACE INTEGRATION (NO FLOATING): The images (character, text, effects) must act as a "texture skin" applied directly to the building's surface. Absolutely NO floating holograms or signboards in mid-air. The projection must follow the physical contours of the architecture.

REALISTIC DISTORTION: The projected light bends around the curved pillars, dips into the window recesses, and reveals the rough grain of the brick texture underneath. The windows and reliefs modify the shape of the projected image.

NO SPILLOVER: The projection is strictly confined to the building's silhouette. The night sky remains pitch black.

场景与构图：
城市中一座巨大的现代化摩天大楼或大型商业设施的超写实夜景照片。从街道斜向上仰视的广角镜头，突出其压倒性的高度和规模。在黑暗的前景中，包括一群仰望的观众。

关键：投影物理与纹理映射：
完整覆盖立面：投影映射覆盖车站整个可见立面，从边缘到边缘。红砖墙的任何部分都不会未被照亮；整个建筑都将变成一个色彩斑斓的游乐园画布。

表面融合（无漂浮）：图像（角色、文本、效果）必须像“纹理皮肤”一样直接应用到建筑表面。绝对不能有漂浮在半空中的全息图或招牌。投影必须遵循建筑的物理轮廓。

真实扭曲：投射的光线会围绕弯曲的柱子弯曲，深入窗户凹槽，并显露出下方砖块粗糙的纹理。窗户和浮雕会改变投射图像的形状。

无溢出：投影严格限制在建筑轮廓内。夜空保持漆黑一片。
