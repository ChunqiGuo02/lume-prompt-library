# 正文 prompt
A moody cinematic dystopian winter scene at dusk, viewed from a sheltered riverside foreground toward a dark modern city skyline glowing with hundreds of tiny warm window lights. In the left foreground, a stylish woman with dark curly hair sits in three-quarter view, her face intentionally obscured by a soft rectangular blur, holding a lit cigarette near her lips. She wears a sheer black lace high-neck dress with intricate floral embroidery, dangling earrings, and a glamorous noir aesthetic. The ground around her is wet, icy, and littered with trash, with glossy black garbage bags piled on the right foreground and scattered debris across the frozen shoreline. Across the icy water in the midground sits a huge weathered metal bowl or basin filled with snow, prominently marked with the text 【USA】. Three tiny human figures stand near it for scale. Bare tree branches frame the upper left. The overall palette is cold blue-gray with amber pinpricks of city light, and the whole image should have a gritty, painterly-photoreal look with a distinct artificial image artifact pattern: a visible grid of tiny glowing specks and dot noise scattered across the entire scene, especially in dark areas, as if from a flawed generative model output.
