On the upper left, add 1 vertical column group of Chinese calligraphy title text in black ink, plus 1 smaller vertical signature line and 2 red seal stamps beneath it. The overall effect should fuse ancient Chinese scholar portraiture with space-age technology and AI symbolism, highly detailed, elegant, balanced, and slightly satirical, with the texture of antique paper and the composition of a museum-quality scroll painting.
