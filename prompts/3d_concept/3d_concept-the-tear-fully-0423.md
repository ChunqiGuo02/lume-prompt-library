# 正文 prompt
Generate an image from the uploaded photo where a realistic person with their natural skin tone and facial features fully preserved emerges through a diamond-shaped tear in plain white wallpaper. The tear rolls outward with soft edges. The person’s hands are raised, and the arms and hands are replaced with black-and-white pencil sketch illustrations that seamlessly extend from the tear, fully matching the realistic pose. Use soft neutral lighting and a simple light-colored background. Keep the composition centered, gender-neutral, with the camera at chest level for a direct, intimate perspective.
