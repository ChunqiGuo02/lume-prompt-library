# 正文 prompt
Using the provided reference image, transform the person into a surreal floating head portrait. Remove the train interior, table, bottle, book, headphones, and all surrounding context. Reframe to a centered, front-facing close-up of only the head, cropped just below the chin, with a glossy hyperreal 3D/plastic look. Replace the background with a bright saturated blue sky containing 【6】 soft fluffy white clouds. Add ornate gold eyeglass temples visible on both sides of the head, and keep the small goatee. Use vivid colors, smooth studio-like lighting, sharp focus on the head, and a whimsical toy-like aesthetic.
