# 正文 prompt
Use case: photorealistic-natural. Create a candid professional photo inside a restaurant kitchen during dinner service. A chef slides a pan across the pass, a line cook reaches for herbs, steam rises from pasta water, and a server waits with two plates. The scene should have natural flow rather than rigid structure: crossed arms, moving hands, steam, stacked plates, towels, and narrow walking space. Camera: 35mm, eye-level, available light, slight motion blur in hands. Avoid staged smiles, logos, unsafe handling, and watermark.
