# 正文 prompt
Ultra-real 16K cinematic scene of two couples on a romantic date on Mars, standing at the edge of a massive red canyon under a glowing violet-red Martian sky. The low sunlight casts long, dramatic shadows across the rust-colored sand dunes. Show subtle dust storms swirling in the distance, illuminated by soft golden light.

The couples wear sleek, minimal futuristic suits with transparent helmets, reflections of the Martian horizon visible on the glass. Their hands touch gently as they walk together, leaving crisp footprints in the thin Martian dust. One couple sits on a metallic bench overlooking the landscape, legs dangling over the cliff, while the other couple takes a slow, weightless walk as if gravity is slightly lower.

Add floating luminescent drones acting as warm lanterns around them, casting soft romantic glows. Include ultra-detailed textures of rocks, dust particles sparkling in the light, and the faint outline of Earth visible as a tiny blue dot in the sky. Capture everything with cinematic depth of field, anamorphic lens streaks, natural color grading, and a completely zero-CGI, photoreal documentary style.

ultra-real, 16K, Mars romance, emotional sci-fi realism, couple silhouettes, atmospheric dust glow, futuristic minimalism, breathtaking cosmic landscape
