# 正文 prompt
Hyper-realistic product transformation video, strict realism, no fantasy.
Locked-off camera, identical framing throughout.
Scene (0.00s – 1.20s):
A premium studio product setup. At the start, a watch is present at center frame. Within the same position, the watch is cleanly removed and replaced by a flat moss/grass texture with identical scale and footprint. The moss surface is static, flat, and texture-based only — no growth, no movement, no spreading, no organic animation.
Constraints:
– Lighting remains unchanged
– Colors remain unchanged
– Depth of field remains unchanged
– Fog, reflections, shadows, and environment remain unchanged
– No cinematic slow motion
– No glowing effects
– No seeds, vines, or plant growth
– No fantasy or surreal elements
– No camera movement
– No environmental interaction
Ultra-sharp hyper-real textures, physically accurate materials, clean studio realism, seamless object replacement, identical atmosphere before and after transformation.
