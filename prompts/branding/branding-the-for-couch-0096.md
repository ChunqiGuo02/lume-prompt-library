# 正文 prompt
Create a clean, square split-screen commercial image for athleisure clothing with polished ad photography and realistic typography. At the top across the full width, place a large bold all-caps white headline in a heavy sans-serif font: 【BOUGHT FOR THE GYM. USED FOR THE COUCH.】 The layout is divided vertically into 2 equal panels. In the left panel, show a neatly arranged flat lay of 5 apparel items on a smooth light gray studio background with soft directional lighting and subtle shadows: 1 black hoodie with drawstrings and a small white swoosh-style chest logo, 1 pair of black athletic shorts with a small white logo near the hem, 1 pair of white crew socks with black swoosh-style logos, and 1 pair of black running shoes with white midsoles and white swoosh-style side logos. The clothing should look premium, minimal, and carefully styled like an e-commerce campaign. In the right panel, show a young man wearing the same black hoodie, black shorts, white crew socks, and black running shoes, slouched lazily on a dark charcoal fabric couch in a dim cozy living room, holding a smartphone and looking relaxed and unmotivated. In front of him on a dark coffee table are 6 visible lounge items: 1 open pizza box with a pizza, 1 red soda can, 1 large red bag of nacho cheese tortilla chips, 1 TV remote, 1 blue bowl of chips, and 1 pink-frosted sprinkled donut partly visible at the bottom edge. Add a draped gray blanket on the couch and keep the mood humorous and relatable. At the bottom center, overlay a smaller white caption in a clean sans-serif font: 【Still worth it.】 High-end product advertising aesthetic, crisp text rendering, brand-aware lifestyle marketing, realistic materials, balanced composition, subtle contrast between aspirational fitness purchase and actual couch use.
