# 正文 prompt
A high-impact product explosion video featuring a red guava soda can centered in frame against a solid red studio background.

SCENE 1 — STILLNESS (0–3 seconds):
The soda can stands perfectly upright and completely still at the center of the frame. No liquid, no fruit, no motion. The can appears sealed, intact, and realistic with light condensation droplets. The background is clean and uniform red. Lighting is steady and neutral. No particles, no splashes, no anticipation effects. Absolute stillness is maintained for a full 3 seconds.

SCENE 2 — IMPACT BURST (instant after 3 seconds):
A violent guava splash erupts from behind the can at extreme speed. The splash is aggressive, sudden, and forceful — not cinematic, not slow motion. Thick red guava liquid explodes forward and upward, slamming into the back of the can and visibly shaking it.

Whole guavas, guava slices, and green leaves are launched outward with raw momentum, bursting through the empty space around the can. Liquid sheets tear through the air, creating sharp splashes, chaotic arcs, and dense droplets. The motion is fast, loud, and explosive.

The can remains mostly upright but reacts physically — shaking, vibrating, and slightly shifting from the impact. Fruit pieces and liquid fragments fill the frame by falling, colliding, and splashing around the can, occupying all previously empty space.

No elegance, no slow motion, no cinematic pacing. The burst feels uncontrolled, pressurized, and powerful. Lighting intensifies during the impact to emphasize liquid thickness, fruit texture, and high-speed motion. Final frames show suspended droplets, falling fruit, and liquid wrapping around the can in chaotic aftermath.

Ultra-realistic physics, extreme speed, aggressive energy, commercial-grade realism, bold contrast, explosive motion — no cinematic softness.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
一个极具冲击力的产品爆炸视频，画面中央是一罐红色番石榴汽水，背景是纯红色摄影棚。

场景 1 — 静止 (0–3 秒)：
汽水罐在画面中央完美直立，完全静止。没有液体，没有水果，没有运动。罐子看起来是密封的，完好无损，逼真地附着着轻微的冷凝水滴。背景干净且统一为红色。灯光稳定而中性。没有颗粒，没有飞溅，没有预示效果。绝对静止保持整整 3 秒。

场景 2 — 冲击爆发 (3 秒后瞬间)：
一股猛烈的番石榴飞溅物以极快的速度从罐子后面喷发而出。飞溅物具有侵略性、突发性和冲击力——不是电影化的，不是慢动作。浓稠的红色番石榴液体向前上方爆炸，猛烈撞击罐子背面并使其明显晃动。

整颗番石榴、番石榴切片和绿叶以原始的动量向外喷射，冲破罐子周围的空白空间。液幕撕裂空气，形成尖锐的飞溅、混乱的弧线和密集的液滴。运动快速、响亮且具有爆炸性。

罐子大部分保持直立，但有物理反应——因冲击而晃动、振动并轻微移动。水果碎片和液体残片通过坠落、碰撞和在罐子周围飞溅而充满画面，占据了之前所有空白的空间。

没有优雅，没有慢动作，没有电影化的节奏。爆发感觉失控、高压且充满力量。冲击期间灯光增强，以强调液体的浓稠度、水果的质感和高速运动。最后几帧显示悬浮的液滴、坠落的水果和液体在混乱的余波中缠绕着罐子。

超逼真的物理效果、极致的速度、强烈的能量、商业级的真实感、大胆的对比、爆炸性的运动——没有电影般的柔和。
