# 正文 prompt
Create a futuristic luxury skincare advertisement poster in a clean editorial layout on a pale icy silver-blue background with subtle metallic texture. Place a single white cylindrical spray bottle in the lower right quadrant, standing upright and casting a long soft blue-gray shadow toward the bottom right. The bottle has a matte premium finish, a rounded shoulder, a white pump top, and minimal dark navy typography. On the bottle label, show the brand name "【SECURE-IP】" near the top, the product name "【IP ADDRESS CLEANSER】" in larger text below, a small line reading "for risk-free connections" with a smaller French line beneath it, and tiny bottom text reading "75 mL / 2.5 fl. oz. - Data Stream Purifier". Emerging from behind and above the bottle, create one dramatic translucent water-like abstract form shaped like a flowing spirit, cresting wave, or liquid ghost, made of glassy blue fluid with iridescent highlights, delicate ribbons, and splashing tendrils sweeping upward and to the right. The liquid sculpture should feel elegant, intelligent, and ethereal, suggesting data streams and hydration. On the left half of the poster, place minimalist navy editorial text with generous spacing: a small brand line reading "【SECURE-IP】" above a large stacked headline reading "【THE FUTURE OF DATA-DRIVEN SKINCARE】". Below that, add one circular outlined arrow icon followed by the words "READ MORE". Near the lower left, include a small caption reading "SAFE CONNECTION" above one thin horizontal progress line ending with "100%". Use a high-end cosmetic campaign aesthetic, ultra-clean composition, lots of negative space, cool monochrome palette, soft diffused lighting, crisp reflections, refined typography, and a polished sci-fi beauty branding mood.

创作一张未来感奢华护肤品广告海报，采用简洁的编辑排版，背景为带有细腻金属质感的淡冰银蓝色。在右下象限放置一个直立的白色圆柱形喷雾瓶，向右下方投射出长而柔和的蓝灰色阴影。瓶身采用哑光高级质感，圆肩设计，配有白色泵头，并印有极简的深海军蓝字体。在瓶身标签上，顶部显示品牌名称 "【SECURE-IP】"，下方用较大字体显示产品名称 "【IP ADDRESS CLEANSER】"，紧接着是一行小字 "for risk-free connections"，下方附有一行更小的法语，底部印有微小的文字 "75 mL / 2.5 fl. oz. - Data Stream Purifier"。在瓶身背后及上方，创作一个极具戏剧性的半透明水状抽象形态，形状如流动的精灵、涌动的波浪或液态幽灵，由带有虹彩高光、精致丝带和向上及向右扫过的飞溅卷须的玻璃质感蓝色流体构成。该液体雕塑应呈现出优雅、智能且空灵的感觉，暗示数据流与补水效果。在海报左半部分，放置极简的海军蓝编辑文本，并留有充裕的间距：顶部是一行小字品牌名 "【SECURE-IP】"，下方是醒目的大号堆叠标题 "【THE FUTURE OF DATA-DRIVEN SKINCARE】"。在标题下方，添加一个圆形轮廓箭头图标，后接 "READ MORE" 字样。在左下角附近，包含一行小标题 "SAFE CONNECTION"，上方是一条细长的水平进度线，末端显示 "100%"。采用高端化妆品广告美学，构图极其简洁，保留大量留白，使用冷色调单色配色，柔和的漫射光，清晰的反射效果，精致的排版，以及考究的科幻美妆品牌氛围。
