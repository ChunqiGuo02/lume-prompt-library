# 正文 prompt
A luxury cosmetics advertisement poster featuring a single upright 【lipstick】 centered on a glossy black cube pedestal against a rich monochrome 【deep crimson red】 studio background. The product is a bold satin-finish 【true red】 lipstick with the bullet fully extended, dramatic sculpted tip, and a two-tone tube: vivid red upper casing and matte black lower casing. On the front of the black base, vertical white brand text reads 【BOBBI BROWN】. Place elegant editorial copy on the left side in a refined high-fashion serif layout, split across multiple lines, reading 【It’s not just a lipstick, it’s an attitude.】, with the final word styled in italic and a slightly brighter coral-red tone than the rest of the text. Add 1 small white logo watermark in the top-right corner reading 【Pollo.ai】. Use cinematic product lighting with soft but high-contrast highlights, subtle reflections on the pedestal, crisp edges, premium beauty-campaign styling, minimalist composition, square format, clean negative space, and a polished ecommerce-ready luxury ad aesthetic.

一张奢华化妆品广告海报，画面中心为一支竖立的 【唇膏】，置于一个光亮的黑色立方体底座上，背景为浓郁的单色 【深绯红色】 工作室背景。产品为一款大胆的丝缎质感 【正红色】 唇膏，膏体完全旋出，呈现出富有戏剧性的雕塑感尖端；管身为双色设计：上部为鲜红色，下部为哑光黑色。在黑色底座正面，印有垂直排列的白色品牌文字 【BOBBI BROWN】。在左侧以精致的高级时装衬线字体排版放置优雅的编辑文案，分多行显示，内容为 【It’s not just a lipstick, it’s an attitude.】，其中最后一个单词采用斜体，色调比其余文字略亮，呈现珊瑚红色。在右上角添加一个小的白色品牌水印 【Pollo.ai】。采用电影级产品布光，具有柔和但高对比度的光影效果，底座上有细腻的倒影，边缘清晰，展现高端美妆广告风格、极简构图、方形画幅、干净的留白以及精致的电商级奢华广告美学。
