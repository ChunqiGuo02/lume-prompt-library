# 正文 prompt
Create a high-resolution square (1:1) food poster of ({"your meal" "YOUR MEAL"}). Capture the dish in a dynamic, explosion-like composition bursting toward the viewer. Present it with realistic, mouthwatering textures and vibrant colors. Place it against a deep black backdrop with dramatic professional studio lighting, rich depth of field, and high contrast.
