# 正文 prompt
A bold Korean beverage advertisement poster in a glossy commercial photography style, featuring 【Donald Trump lookalike】 in a dark navy business suit, white dress shirt, and vivid blue tie, standing front-facing against a saturated bright-blue sky with a few soft white clouds and a faint modern city skyline including a tall skyscraper resembling the Empire State Building on the right. He is holding a cold plastic bottle of 【POCARI SWEAT】 dramatically toward the camera in his left hand, with extreme foreshortening so the bottle appears large in the foreground and the person is slightly behind it. The bottle is clear with condensation, a white cap, visible water inside, and a deep blue label reading “POCARI SWEAT” in large white letters with smaller text above saying “ION SUPPLY DRINK.” Add energetic frozen water splashes and droplets around the bottle, hand, and lower foreground to emphasize freshness and hydration. Place the brand logo in the upper left corner: “POCARI SWEAT” in white with the curved underline mark. Across the top, add huge distressed white Korean headline text, slightly angled and filling most of the upper half: 【갈증 해소? 이걸로 끝! 포카리스웨트!】. In the lower right over the suit, add a bold italic quotation in white and blue Korean text: 【WINNING은 수분 싸움! 포카리스웨트로 리프레시!】. In the bottom left, include exactly 3 circular benefit icons stacked vertically with short Korean labels: a water drop icon with “수분 보충”, a lightning bolt icon with “전해질 보충”, and a running figure icon with “빠른 흡수”. Use a high-contrast, patriotic blue-and-white color palette, dramatic ad lighting, sharp focus on the bottle and hand, slightly softer focus on the face and background, and an overall energetic premium campaign-poster look.
