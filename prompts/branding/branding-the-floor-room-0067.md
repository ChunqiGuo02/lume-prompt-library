# 正文 prompt
A darkly comedic, cinematic product advertisement staged in a messy modern living room at night. In the background, a person in a full 【Spider-Man-inspired red and blue suit】 lies face-down and motionless on a wooden floor, one arm stretched toward the camera, posed like a defeated superhero after a battle. The room is dim and moody with a gray couch in soft focus, pillows and blankets tossed around, and scattered white popcorn across the floor. In the left foreground, show 1 open pizza box cropped into frame. In the right foreground, place 1 large upright can of 【Raid】 insect spray as the hero object, sharply focused, oversized in perspective, with a black cap and a purple label facing camera. Make the product feel dominant, clean, and commercial, with dramatic rim lighting and glossy reflections. Add 2 lines of bold white uppercase ad copy: at the top, very large condensed sans-serif text reading 【NOT EVEN A SUPERHERO SURVIVES.】; at the bottom center, smaller bold text reading 【Raid. Kills bugs dead.】. Include 2 visible insect illustrations on the can label, and keep the overall tone like a high-end shock-value commercial campaign: realistic photography, shallow depth of field, strong contrast, gritty floor texture, subdued gray-blue interior lighting, and a composition that makes the spray can the clear focal point while the fallen hero tells the joke in the background.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
一则黑色幽默风格的电影质感产品广告，场景设定在夜晚凌乱的现代客厅中。背景中，一名身穿 【蜘蛛侠风格红蓝战衣】 的人脸朝下瘫倒在木地板上，一动不动，一只手臂伸向镜头，呈现出超级英雄战败后的姿态。房间光线昏暗，氛围压抑，背景中灰色的沙发处于虚焦状态，枕头和毯子散落一地，地板上撒满了白色爆米花。左侧前景处展示一个被裁剪进画面的披萨盒。右侧前景处放置一罐巨大的、直立的 【Raid】 杀虫剂作为主角，焦点清晰，透视比例夸张，黑色瓶盖，紫色标签正对镜头。使产品呈现出主导、整洁且商业化的质感，带有戏剧性的轮廓光和光泽反射。添加两行醒目的白色大写广告文案：顶部使用超大号紧凑型无衬线字体，内容为 【NOT EVEN A SUPERHERO SURVIVES.】；底部居中处使用较小的粗体文字，内容为 【Raid. Kills bugs dead.】。在瓶身标签上包含 2 个可见的昆虫插图，整体基调保持高端视觉冲击力商业广告风格：写实摄影、浅景深、强对比度、粗糙的地板纹理、柔和的灰蓝色室内光线，构图使杀虫剂成为绝对的视觉焦点，而背景中倒下的英雄则起到幽默讽刺的效果。
