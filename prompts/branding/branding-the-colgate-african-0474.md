# 正文 prompt
Luxury advertising poster for 【Colgate Sensation White】, split vertically into two mirrored halves by a narrow ornate mirror with a bright blue glow at the center. On the left side, an elderly African woman in three-quarter profile stands with arms folded, wearing a richly patterned indigo-and-gold traditional wax-print dress, a matching wrapped headscarf, layered gold bead necklaces, drop earrings, stacked bracelets, and holding 1 traditional chewing stick upright in one hand. On the right side, a younger African woman in matching indigo-and-gold dress and gold jewelry mirrors the pose and holds 1 modern white-and-blue toothbrush upright in one hand. Both figures are elegant, dignified, and photographed from the waist up against a warm textured brown studio backdrop with cinematic golden lighting, high contrast, polished skin tones, and premium beauty-ad aesthetics. Add 1 red circular Colgate logo in the top left corner. Overlay large refined serif typography in cream across the middle-lower area: left text 【Depuis toujours.】 and right text 【Pour toujours.】, with a glowing blue tooth icon centered between the two phrases on the mirror divide. Add a smaller centered serif tagline near the bottom in cream: 【Le meilleur de la tradition, la force de la science】. Place 1 Colgate Sensation White toothpaste box in the bottom right corner, angled slightly toward camera, glossy and realistic. The overall composition should communicate the fusion of ancestral tradition and modern dental science, premium African fashion portraiture, symmetrical balance, editorial campaign design, and a sophisticated international cosmetics advertisement.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
为 【Colgate Sensation White】 设计的奢侈品广告海报，画面被中间一个带有明亮蓝光的精致窄镜垂直分为左右两半。左侧，一位非洲老妇人侧身四分之三面朝镜头，双臂交叉，身穿图案华丽的靛蓝配金色传统蜡染印花裙，佩戴配套的头巾、多层金珠项链、垂坠耳环和叠戴手镯，手中垂直握着 1 根传统咀嚼棒。右侧，一位身穿同款靛蓝配金色裙装并佩戴金饰的年轻非洲女性，以同样的姿势镜像站立，手中垂直握着 1 支现代蓝白配色牙刷。两位人物均优雅端庄，半身像拍摄，背景为温暖的棕色纹理影棚背景，配以电影质感的金色灯光、高对比度、细腻的肤色处理及高端美妆广告美学。在左上角添加 1 个红色圆形 Colgate 标志。在画面中下部叠加大型精致的奶油色衬线字体：左侧文字为 【Depuis toujours.】，右侧文字为 【Pour toujours.】，镜面分割线中间放置一个发光的蓝色牙齿图标。在底部中央添加一行较小的奶油色衬线标语：【Le meilleur de la tradition, la force de la science】。在右下角放置 1 盒 Colgate Sensation White 牙膏，略微朝向镜头，质感光亮且真实。整体构图旨在传达祖传传统与现代牙科科学的融合，展现高端非洲时尚人像、对称平衡感、编辑级广告设计以及国际化的高级化妆品广告风格。
