# 正文 prompt
Create a premium minimalist keynote-style presentation cover slide in an Apple-inspired industrial design aesthetic. Use a wide 16:9 layout with a deep matte black upper background and a pale concrete-gray lower platform occupying the bottom quarter of the frame. Place a large photorealistic floating rectangular slab in the right-center foreground, resembling a thin black glass device or polished obsidian platform with mirror-like reflections, chamfered chrome-metal edges, and precise industrial finishing. The slab should hover above the light surface and cast a soft elongated shadow directly beneath it. Add a tall vertical gray metallic wall or pillar on the far right edge to frame the composition. Use extremely sparse, high-end art direction, lots of negative space, cinematic studio lighting, subtle gradients, and crisp contrast between black and silver materials. On the left side, set clean white sans-serif typography with a clear hierarchy: a tiny top caption reading "【Apple-inspired presentation system】", a very large bold headline reading "【Clarity At Scale】", and a medium subtitle reading "【The strongest slides behave like products.】". Below that, include exactly 3 numbered principles in a vertical list with thin separators and refined spacing: "01 | 【One focal object】", "02 | Binary light / dark rhythm", and "03 | Blue only for signal". Add a short thin horizontal rule above the list and a tiny blue accent line below the list. Draw a very fine horizontal guide line near the top spanning most of the slide, with the small page number "01" at the upper right. Repeat the small caption "Apple-inspired presentation system" once again at the bottom left on the light surface. Overall look: luxury tech presentation, editorial product-launch slide, ultra-clean Swiss grid, restrained monochrome palette with only one subtle blue accent, photorealistic rendering blended with presentation design.
