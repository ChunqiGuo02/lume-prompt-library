# 正文 prompt
Luxury fashion editorial collage poster for 【LOEWE】, vertical composition, moody autumn earth-tone palette, designed like a premium magazine advertisement made from layered torn-paper fragments. In the center, a waist-up female fashion model with wild windswept dark wavy hair wears a rich cognac-brown belted leather coat with a high collar and soft natural folds, posed slightly turned to the side in a dramatic fashion stance. Her face is partially obscured and de-emphasized, with the styling and garment taking focus. Surrounding her are 8 clearly visible collage fragments: 1 central model portrait, 1 top-left dark textured fabric swatch, 1 upper-left rocky desert landscape fragment, 1 upper-center black circular moon or planet against cloudy sky, 1 upper-right leafless tree silhouette, 1 right-side gray textile swatch with part of a brown leather strap and metal ring, 1 left-lower brown leather sample embossed with an anagram logo, 1 lower-right cream fringed fabric patch embroidered with the same logo. Add an additional soft beige paper layer at the bottom left to increase depth. Use torn deckled edges everywhere, overlapping cut-paper assembly, tactile material contrast, and subtle shadows between layers. Background is a stormy cloudy sky blended with barren landscape, evoking raw nature and artisanal craftsmanship. Place large elegant white serif text 【LOEWE】 across the lower middle over the coat, centered and spanning most of the width. Include a refined embossed monogram logo on the leather sample and embroidered monogram on the fabric patch. Lighting is soft, cinematic, and diffused, with high texture detail in leather, cloth, paper fibers, and landscape. Overall mood: sophisticated, tactile, artistic, premium luxury campaign, handcrafted collage, fashion house advertisement, high-end print editorial.
