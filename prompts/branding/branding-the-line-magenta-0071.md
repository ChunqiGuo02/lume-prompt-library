# 正文 prompt
Create a bold static SaaS ad on a matte black background with a premium editorial look and highly accurate typography. At the top, place a large stacked serif headline in off-white reading: "Your analytics stack" on the first line, "shouldn’t cost more" on the second line in bright magenta italic serif, and "than your rent." on the third line in off-white serif. Center the composition around a large curled, crumpled receipt made of white thermal paper, angled slightly from upper left to lower right, with visible paper texture, soft shadows, and rolled ends on both the upper right and lower left. On the receipt, show exactly 5 line items in black monospaced or receipt-style text aligned in two columns: "GA4" with "$0", "Hotjar" with "$99", "Optimizely" with "$2,450", "GTM setup (dev hours)" with "$1,800", and "Tag manager debug" with "$400". Highlight the word "Optimizely" with a jagged magenta starburst sticker behind it. Add a dashed divider line below the items, then a bold "TOTAL" label on the lower left of the receipt. On the lower right of the receipt, place a large price reading "【$19/mo】" in heavy black type, circled with 2 hand-drawn magenta marker loops. Add 3 handwritten magenta annotation elements: on the left side, the words "【one tool】" in brush script with an underline and a curved arrow pointing to the receipt; near the bottom center, a curved magenta arrow pointing upward toward the circled price; and at the bottom right, the handwritten tagline "【One script. One price.】" with an underline. In the bottom-right corner, add a small block of white sans-serif body copy, right-aligned, reading "【Analytics, heatmaps, A/B testing, and revenue attribution.】". Use an editorial ad-photography style with crisp text placement, clean hierarchy, high contrast, subtle paper realism, and a limited palette of black, white, and hot pink.
