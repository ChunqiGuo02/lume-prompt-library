Apply the exact brand palette and overall visual identity from the reference: deep black background, red accent, white typography, charcoal overlays, and clean modern sans-serif styling. Use bold billboard copy reading 【NETFLIX VR】, 【COMING SOON】, and 【28TH MAY】. Add small UI chips and labels on the right summarizing the prompt, plus generation-time badges above each of the 4 images.
