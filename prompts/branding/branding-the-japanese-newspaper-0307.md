# 正文 prompt
A realistic smartphone screenshot of the ChatGPT web interface in Japanese, shown on a clean white background. Centered in the chat is a single generated image: a top-down photo of 1 Japanese newspaper placed neatly on a medium-dark wooden tabletop with rounded table corners visible. The newspaper dominates the image and looks highly believable, like a fake but convincing breaking-news front page. The masthead area is dense and traditional, with a huge bold black Japanese headline across the top reading 「高市総理辞任」. Beneath it is a large main photo of 1 politician at a press conference podium with multiple microphones, face intentionally blurred or obscured, in front of a dark blue curtain. Along the right side is 1 vertical blue newspaper nameplate area with white Japanese text resembling a major national daily. A tall vertical subheadline column in Japanese runs beside the main image, suggesting discussion of a successor candidate. The front page includes exactly 4 visible advertisement blocks near the bottom and lower side areas, 1 small portrait photo of a suited man in a left-side article, 1 sports photo block in the lower middle, 1 weather or data box on the right, and many tightly packed columns of Japanese body text throughout. The newspaper should feel authentic, serious, and editorial, with realistic print texture, slight page curl, subtle shadows, and credible Japanese typography. Around the image, include the visible ChatGPT UI chrome: top-left “ChatGPT”, top-right Japanese sharing controls, a small circular overlay button labeled 「編集」 at the lower-left corner of the image, a small share-style overlay icon at the lower-right corner of the image, reaction/action icons beneath the image, and a bottom input bar with Japanese placeholder text 「質問してみましょう」 plus microphone and voice buttons. Overall style: photorealistic screenshot, modern web app interface, fake-news newspaper front page, crisp legible layout, natural lighting, minimal perspective distortion.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
一张逼真的 ChatGPT 日语网页版智能手机截图，背景为简洁的白色。聊天界面中央是一张生成的图片：一张从正上方拍摄的照片，展示了 1 份整齐放置在深色木质桌面上的日本报纸，可见圆润的桌面边缘。报纸占据了画面的主体，看起来非常真实，像是一份虚构但令人信服的突发新闻头版。报头区域布局紧凑且传统，顶部印有醒目的黑色粗体日语标题「高市総理辞任」。下方是一张大型主图，展示了 1 位在新闻发布会讲台上的政治人物，面前有多个麦克风，面部被刻意模糊或遮挡，背景为深蓝色幕布。右侧是 1 个垂直的蓝色报纸名称栏，带有类似日本主流全国性日报的白色日语文字。主图旁有一列垂直的日语副标题栏，暗示了对继任候选人的讨论。报纸头版底部及侧下方包含 4 个清晰可见的广告块，左侧文章中配有 1 张身着西装男士的小型肖像照，下方中间有 1 个体育新闻图片块，右侧有 1 个天气或数据框，整版充斥着密集的日语正文列。报纸应呈现出真实、严肃的编辑风格，具有逼真的印刷质感、轻微的纸张卷曲、细腻的阴影以及可信的日语排版。图片周围应包含可见的 ChatGPT UI 元素：左上角的“ChatGPT”、右上角的日语分享控件、图片左下角标有「編集」的小型圆形浮层按钮、图片右下角的小型分享图标、图片下方的反应/操作图标，以及底部的输入栏，其中包含日语占位符文字「質問してみましょう」以及麦克风和语音按钮。整体风格：照片级真实截图、现代 Web 应用界面、虚假新闻报纸头版、清晰易读的布局、自然光影、极小的透视畸变。
