# 正文 prompt
Create a high-end futuristic skincare advertisement poster in icy blue and white tones, vertical 2:3 composition. The scene features a translucent glass female bust in left profile with a low bun hairstyle, occupying the left two-thirds of the frame. Inside the transparent head, neck, and torso is a surreal miniature landscape of blue cliffs, glowing water, and multiple waterfalls, like a frozen digital sanctuary contained within the body. Add tiny suspended bubbles, glossy reflections, misty atmosphere, and soft aurora-like light streaks drifting diagonally across the image. On the right side, place a clean white cylindrical skincare bottle standing upright on a jagged translucent ice pedestal. The bottle label should read 【SECURE-IP】 and below it 【IP ADDRESS CLEANSER】, with smaller technical cosmetic copy beneath in a minimal editorial layout. Set giant vertical glowing text along the far right edge reading 【DEEP CORE PURITY】. Add 4 small infographic-style callouts in a sleek tech-beauty design: a top-right droplet icon with the heading “MOISTURE LOCK” and two short lines of tiny supporting text, a mid-right shield icon with the heading “IP PROTECTION” and three short lines of tiny supporting text, a lower-left headline block reading “PURE CONNECTION PURE CONFIDENCE” with 2 short lines of body copy, and a far-left small label reading “DATA STREAM PURIFIER” with a bracket line pointing toward the glass figure. Include a centered solid pale blue rectangular overlay covering part of the face and upper torso, as a flat opaque block. Lighting is ethereal, glossy, and premium, with strong bloom, frosted highlights, soft backlight, and luxury sci-fi cosmetic campaign styling. Ultra-detailed, photorealistic 3D render, clean commercial poster design, minimal yet surreal, polished surfaces, cold luminous atmosphere.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
创作一张高端未来感护肤广告海报，采用冰蓝色与白色调，构图比例为 2:3 竖版。画面左侧三分之二处为一位半透明玻璃质感的女性半身像，侧面朝左，梳着低发髻。在透明的头部、颈部和躯干内部，是一个超现实的微缩景观，包含蓝色悬崖、发光的水流和多条瀑布，宛如一个封存在体内的冰冻数字圣域。添加细小的悬浮气泡、光亮的反射、朦胧的氛围，以及斜向穿过画面的柔和极光状光束。在右侧，放置一个干净的白色圆柱形护肤瓶，直立在锯齿状的半透明冰座上。瓶身标签应显示 【SECURE-IP】，下方为 【IP ADDRESS CLEANSER】，并在下方以极简的杂志排版风格添加较小的技术性化妆品文案。在最右侧边缘设置巨大的垂直发光文字，内容为 【DEEP CORE PURITY】。添加 4 个小型信息图表风格的标注，采用流畅的科技美妆设计：右上角为水滴图标，标题为“MOISTURE LOCK”，下方配有两行简短的辅助文字；中右侧为盾牌图标，标题为“IP PROTECTION”，下方配有三行简短的辅助文字；左下角为标题块，内容为“PURE CONNECTION PURE CONFIDENCE”，配有 2 行简短正文；最左侧有一个小标签，内容为“DATA STREAM PURIFIER”，并带有指向玻璃人像的括号线。在面部和上半身覆盖一个居中的浅蓝色实心矩形遮罩，作为平面的不透明色块。光影效果空灵、光亮且高级，具有强烈的辉光、磨砂高光、柔和的背光以及奢华的科幻美妆大片风格。超精细、照片级 3D 渲染，干净的商业海报设计，极简而超现实，表面光洁，营造出寒冷而明亮的氛围。
