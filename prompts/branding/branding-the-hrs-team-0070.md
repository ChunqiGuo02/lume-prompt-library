# 正文 prompt
Create a minimalist static SaaS ad on a warm off-white background with elegant editorial typography and clean, high-fidelity text rendering. At the top, place a large black serif headline in two lines reading: "【Your dev team shouldn’t be your analytics team.】" The words “dev team” should appear in italic serif while the rest remains bold high-contrast serif, with generous spacing and a luxury magazine-style layout. Centered below, show a slightly tilted white paper invoice sheet with a soft drop shadow and a subtle vertical fold crease down the middle, as if photographed from above. The invoice uses black monospaced typewriter-style text and reads exactly: “INVOICE — DEV HOURS, OCTOBER” as the header, then 5 line items with hours and prices aligned in columns: “A/B test setup on pricing page   8 hrs   $1,200”, “GTM tag install (3 events)   4 hrs   $600”, “Funnel tracking debug   6 hrs   $900”, “Meta pixel mapping   3 hrs   $450”, and “Attribution reporting build   12 hrs   $1,800”. Under the 5 items, add a dashed divider line, then a bold monospaced “TOTAL” on the lower left and the total price “$4,950” on the lower right. Draw a hand-drawn yellow line striking through the total price. Add 4 doodle-style annotation elements around the invoice: on the left, handwritten yellow text saying “every month??” with a yellow arrow pointing right toward the paper; on the upper right of the paper, a yellow starburst scribble; below the total, a green curved arrow pointing up toward the crossed-out price; and on the lower right, a green hand-drawn circle containing the handwritten words “zero devs,” connected to the green arrow. At the bottom center of the ad, add a smaller black sans-serif body copy in 2 lines reading: “【Marketers ship tests and track revenue themselves.\nNo tickets. No tag manager. One script.】" Keep the composition airy, premium, and conversion-focused, blending modern startup ad design with tactile paper realism and playful marker annotations.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
创建一个极简风格的静态 SaaS 广告，背景为温暖的米白色，采用优雅的编辑排版和清晰的高保真文本渲染。在顶部放置一个两行的大号黑色衬线标题，内容为：“【Your dev team shouldn’t be your analytics team.】”。其中 “dev team” 一词应使用斜体衬线字体，其余部分保持粗体高对比度衬线字体，并采用宽敞的留白和奢华杂志风格布局。在下方居中位置，展示一张略微倾斜的白色纸质发票，带有柔和的投影和中间细微的垂直折痕，如同从上方拍摄。发票使用黑色等宽打字机风格文本，标题明确写着：“INVOICE — DEV HOURS, OCTOBER”，随后是 5 行对齐的工时与价格明细：“A/B test setup on pricing page   8 hrs   $1,200”、“GTM tag install (3 events)   4 hrs   $600”、“Funnel tracking debug   6 hrs   $900”、“Meta pixel mapping   3 hrs   $450” 以及 “Attribution reporting build   12 hrs   $1,800”。在 5 个项目下方添加一条虚线分隔符，左下方写上粗体等宽的 “TOTAL”，右下方写上总价 “$4,950”。用手绘黄色线条划掉总价。在发票周围添加 4 个涂鸦风格的注释元素：左侧是手写的黄色文字 “every month??”，并配有一个指向纸张的黄色箭头；纸张右上角有一个黄色星形涂鸦；总价下方有一个指向被划掉价格的绿色弯曲箭头；右下角是一个绿色的手绘圆圈，里面写着手写文字 “zero devs”，并与绿色箭头相连。在广告底部中心，添加两行较小的黑色无衬线正文，内容为：“【Marketers ship tests and track revenue themselves.\nNo tickets. No tag manager. One script.】”。保持构图通透、高级且以转化为导向，将现代初创公司广告设计与真实的纸张质感及趣味标记注释融为一体。
