# 正文 prompt
Create a clean minimalist static ad on an off-white light gray studio background with a centered editorial layout. At the top, place a large luxury-fashion-style serif headline in black reading 【Stop gambling with your ad spend.】, with the word "gambling" set in italic serif for emphasis. In the middle, show a vintage metal slot-machine window viewed straight on, slightly weathered and realistic, with 3 vertical reels inside a rectangular steel housing and a lever with a black knob on the right side. The 3 reels must display, from left to right, "$", "$", and "?". Make the rightmost reel background pale pink while the first 2 reels are aged white. Add a hand-drawn neon-pink annotation style over the image: 1 arrow-and-note on the left pointing to the first reel reading 【your attribution】, 1 starburst scribble above the pink question-mark reel, 1 arrow from the question-mark reel to a circled note on the right reading 【actual revenue】, and 1 large curved arrow in the lower left pointing toward the body copy area. Below the slot machine, center a short sans-serif paragraph in black reading 【See which campaigns drive revenue, not just clicks. Connect Meta, Google, and Stripe in 60 seconds.】. At the bottom center, place a simple black analytics-style bar logo icon followed by the bold sans-serif brand name 【Humblytics】. Use strong text fidelity, polished product-ad composition, soft studio shadows, restrained palette of black, steel gray, off-white, and hot pink, and a modern direct-response ad aesthetic.

Connect Meta, Google, and Stripe in 60 seconds.】。底部居中放置一个简单的黑色分析风格条形图 Logo 图标，后跟加粗的无衬线品牌名称 【Humblytics】。要求文字清晰度高，产品广告构图精致，具有柔和的摄影棚阴影，配色方案限制在黑色、钢灰色、灰白色和亮粉色，并呈现现代直接响应式广告的美感。
