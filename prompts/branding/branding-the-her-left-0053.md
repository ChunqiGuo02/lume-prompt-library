# 正文 prompt
A luxury fashion editorial advertisement poster in a vertical smartphone format, centered on a female model from chest-up wearing a structured olive green leather coat with a sharp pointed collar and a single visible round brown button at the neck. Her dark wet-look hair is slicked back and slightly messy, her head tilted upward with an elegant elongated neck, and her face mostly obscured by framing and shadow to create a mysterious high-fashion mood. Across the center, place the brand name 【LOEWE】 in very large white high-contrast serif capital letters, spanning nearly the full width of the image. Build the composition as a collage framed by 5 torn material swatches: 1 embossed olive suede piece in the top left with a luxury monogram pressed into it, 1 coarse taupe-beige canvas fabric piece in the top right attached to a warm tan leather strip with visible white stitching, 1 pale textured fabric scrap with frayed edges in the bottom left, 1 dark brown glossy leather piece in the bottom right with a subtle embossed monogram, and 1 narrow vertical weathered beige panel along the left edge. Inside that left panel, include thin dark desert plant stems with small seed heads. In the background behind the model, use a dramatic golden-brown sunset sky with soft clouds and a faint rocky desert landscape near the lower right, creating a warm earthy palette. Lighting should be cinematic, moody, and directional, with rich shadows, tactile material detail, premium fashion-magazine styling, and an elegant artisan-luxury aesthetic. Use a color palette of olive green, camel, tan, beige, chocolate brown, and sunset gold. The overall feeling is sophisticated, handcrafted, minimal, and expensive, like a heritage luxury leather-goods campaign.

一张竖版手机格式的奢华时尚编辑广告海报，画面中心为一位女性模特半身像，身穿结构感橄榄绿皮衣，配有尖领，颈部可见一颗圆形的棕色纽扣。她深色的湿发造型向后梳理，略显凌乱，头部向上倾斜，颈部线条优雅修长，面部大部分被边框和阴影遮挡，营造出神秘的高级时尚氛围。在画面中心，放置品牌名称 【LOEWE】，使用超大号白色高对比度衬线大写字母，几乎横跨整个图像宽度。构图采用由 5 片撕裂材质拼贴而成的边框：左上角为一片压印有奢华字母组合的橄榄色麂皮，右上角为一片粗糙的灰褐色帆布，连接着一条带有明显白色缝线的暖棕色皮带，左下角为一片边缘磨损的浅色纹理织物碎片，右下角为一片带有细微压印字母组合的深棕色亮面皮革，左侧边缘为一条狭窄的垂直风化米色面板。在该左侧面板内，包含细长的深色沙漠植物茎秆及小型种子头。模特背景为戏剧性的金棕色日落天空，伴有柔和的云层，右下角隐约可见岩石沙漠景观，营造出温暖的大地色调。灯光应具有电影感、情绪化且具备方向性，呈现丰富的阴影、细腻的材质细节、高级时尚杂志风格以及优雅的工匠奢华美学。色彩搭配采用橄榄绿、驼色、棕褐色、米色、巧克力棕和日落金。整体感觉精致、手工感强、极简且昂贵，如同传承深厚的奢华皮革制品广告活动。
