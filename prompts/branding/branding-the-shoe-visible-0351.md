# 正文 prompt
Create a cinematic sports advertising poster featuring a giant close-up running shoe in motion splashing through shallow water on a wet city street at sunset. The composition is a low-angle, ultra-dynamic action shot with 1 primary shoe dominating the right half of the frame and 1 secondary blurred shoe visible behind it, implying a runner mid-stride. The hero sneaker is a white and gray knit performance shoe with a black side swoosh-style logo, gray laces, small red lace-loop accents, a thick translucent cushioned sole, and a bright orange-red outsole glow along the bottom edge. Show the shoe covered in realistic water droplets and sharp texture detail, with explosive splash particles frozen around the sole. The runner wears dark athletic leggings, visible only from the lower legs down. Place a dramatic urban skyline in the distant background on the right, softly blurred, under stormy dark clouds breaking into warm golden sunlight near the horizon. Use intense contrast between cool blue-gray shadows and fiery orange highlights, with glossy reflections on the rain-soaked pavement. On the left side, add 3 text elements arranged vertically: a huge bold distressed white uppercase headline reading 【JUST DO IT.】, beneath it 1 white swoosh-style brand mark, and below that a smaller uppercase orange motivational line reading 【YOU CAN'T STOP WHAT'S ALREADY INSIDE YOU.】. Include visible branding on the tongue label with 【NIKE】. Make it look like a premium high-impact commercial poster for 【running shoes】, with hyperreal detail, dramatic lighting, shallow depth of field, motion energy, crisp typography, ad-photography polish, and a widescreen 16:9 layout.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
创作一张电影感运动鞋广告海报，画面主体为一只巨大的跑步鞋特写，正处于运动状态，在日落时分的潮湿城市街道上溅起浅水。构图采用低角度、超动态的动作捕捉视角，一只主鞋占据画面右侧，另一只模糊的鞋在后方，暗示跑者正处于跨步中。这款主打运动鞋为白灰针织材质，侧面带有黑色勾形标志，配有灰色鞋带、红色小鞋带扣点缀、厚实的半透明缓冲鞋底，以及鞋底边缘发出的亮橙红色光芒。展示鞋面上逼真的水珠和清晰的纹理细节，鞋底周围冻结着爆炸般的溅水颗粒。跑者穿着深色运动紧身裤，仅露出小腿以下部分。背景右侧远方是柔焦处理的城市天际线，在乌云密布的天空中，地平线附近透出温暖的金色阳光。运用冷蓝灰色阴影与火热橙色高光之间的强烈对比，路面呈现出雨后湿润的质感。在左侧垂直排列 3 个文本元素：一个巨大的粗体做旧白色大写标题 【JUST DO IT.】，下方是一个白色勾形品牌标志，再下方是一行较小的橙色大写励志标语 【YOU CAN'T STOP WHAT'S ALREADY INSIDE YOU.】。在鞋舌标签上加入可见的品牌名称 【NIKE】。使其看起来像是一张针对 【running shoes】 的高端高冲击力商业海报，具备超写实细节、戏剧性光影、浅景深、运动能量感、清晰的排版、广告摄影级的质感，以及 16:9 宽屏布局。
