# 正文 prompt
A vintage travel poster illustration for 【MALTA】, with the subtitle 【MEDITERRANEAN PARADISE】 centered near the top. The composition is a sunlit Mediterranean coastal cityscape viewed from a curving seaside road in the foreground, painted in a richly textured retro poster style with warm cream stone architecture, saturated turquoise-blue sea, and a bright azure sky with soft white clouds. On the left, a hillside of tightly packed historic limestone buildings rises above the waterfront, including colorful enclosed wooden balconies, a large domed church with a reddish terracotta roof, and a slender church spire. In the foreground on the road, place a classic mid-century white sedan seen from the rear three-quarter angle, driving along the curve, with a dark license plate reading 【MTA 1964】. A low stone seawall runs along the right edge of the road beside the harbor. In the bay, include exactly 4 boats: 2 colorful Maltese luzzu fishing boats in the near and mid water, plus 2 smaller distant boats farther back. Add exactly 4 white seagulls gliding across the sky above the water. Frame the scene with lush flowering bougainvillea branches in vivid magenta-pink arching across the upper right and lower right corners, and leafy tree branches along the upper left edge; include a spiky agave plant in the lower left foreground. The distant shoreline across the bay should show a pale sunlit city stretching along the horizon. Use a nostalgic 1950s–1960s tourism poster aesthetic, painterly brushwork, crisp composition, romantic travel mood, and elegant typography with the main title in large cream serif capitals and the subtitle in smaller dark sans-serif capitals.

一张复古旅行海报插画，主题为 【MALTA】，副标题 【MEDITERRANEAN PARADISE】 居中置于顶部附近。画面构图为从前景弯曲的海滨公路望向阳光明媚的地中海沿岸城市，采用纹理丰富的复古海报风格绘制，呈现出温暖的奶油色石质建筑、饱和的绿松石色大海，以及点缀着柔和白云的蔚蓝天空。左侧，密集的历史石灰岩建筑群沿山坡向上延伸，包括色彩鲜艳的封闭式木质阳台、一座带有红陶屋顶的大型圆顶教堂以及一座细长的教堂尖塔。在前景的公路上，放置一辆 20 世纪中叶的经典白色轿车，从后侧方视角呈现，正沿弯道行驶，车牌为深色，文字为 【MTA 1964】。公路右侧边缘沿海港设有一道低矮的石质防波堤。海湾内包含 4 艘船：近处和中景处各有一艘色彩斑斓的马耳他 Luzzu 渔船，远处还有 2 艘较小的船只。天空中飞翔着 4 只白色的海鸥。画面四周点缀着繁茂的三角梅枝叶，鲜艳的洋红色花朵在右上角和右下角拱起，左上角有茂密的树枝；左下角前景处设有一株尖刺状的龙舌兰植物。海湾对岸的远景展现了一座在阳光下泛着淡光的城市沿地平线延伸。采用 20 世纪 50 年代至 60 年代的怀旧旅游海报美学，运用绘画笔触、清晰的构图、浪漫的旅行氛围，以及优雅的排版，主标题采用大型奶油色衬线大写字母，副标题采用较小的深色无衬线大写字母。
