# 正文 prompt
A fashion magazine cover styled like a luxurious Japanese women's magazine, photographed in soft natural daylight against a pale blue sky with a faint city skyline blurred in the background. The masthead is huge and elegant across the top, reading 【Lumiére】 in oversized soft pink serif lettering, with small Japanese cover text above and around it, plus a small issue marker on the upper right reading "5", "May 2026", and "No.218". Center the cover on exactly 2 young adult Japanese women standing close together from about thigh-up, facing camera in a poised editorial pose, with their faces intentionally obscured by smooth soft rectangular blur blocks. The woman on the left wears a flowing pale blue dress inspired by koinobori carp streamers: high Mandarin-style collar, sleeveless inner dress, long sheer cape panels draping from the shoulders, elaborate layered metallic and satin fish-scale appliqué across the bodice, a large stylized fish eye motif on the chest, more scale detailing and fin-shaped ornamentation at the hip, and a cinched waist with decorative jeweled clasp. Her dark hair is in an elegant updo with loose wisps, and she wears dangling earrings. The woman on the right wears the same design concept in pale pink and white, also with a high collar, translucent cape-like side panels, layered fish-scale bodice, a visible fish eye motif near the waist sash, soft pleated skirt, tassel ornament at the belt, and matching dangling earrings; her dark hair is also swept into a refined updo with a few loose strands. The overall styling is airy, graceful, feminine, and sophisticated, mixing traditional Japanese elegance with modern couture. Use a pastel palette dominated by baby blue, blush pink, white, and subtle gold trim, with bright but diffused lighting and a premium glossy magazine editorial finish. Include visible Japanese cover lines arranged like a real magazine layout: one left-side block introducing the cover girls, one large main headline block across the lower half, one right-side vertical cluster of smaller teaser text, and exactly 2 pale blue circular callout badges on the right side containing Japanese text; the upper badge advertises a special appendix gift set and includes exactly 2 small product images of pouch-like accessories, and the lower badge contains a shopping-plan checklist with exactly 3 checkbox lines. Add a small pink ribbon-like label above the main headline. The composition should feel clean, aspirational, and seasonally themed around Golden Week and koinobori fashion.
