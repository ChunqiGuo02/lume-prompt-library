# 正文 prompt
Do this for {argument name="city" default="New York"}: System Instruction:
Generate a hyper-realistic 3D architectural typography render. The letters must act as massive, hollow containers housing a "Micro-World" that semantically matches the Input Word.
1. Semantic Inference (The Blueprint):
Analyze the Input Word to determine the Biome, Material, and Lighting:
If Organic (e.g., JUNGLE, LIFE): Material = Glass & Wood. Interior = Vines, Waterfalls, Trees. Light = Sunlight/Green.
If Urban (e.g., CITY, NYC): Material = Steel & Concrete. Interior = Skyscrapers, Traffic, Streetlights. Light = Amber/Blue.
If Industrial (e.g., FACTORY, RUST): Material = Rusted Iron & Mesh. Interior = Pipes, Steam, Gears. Light = Orange Furnace glow.
If Abstract (e.g., LOVE, HOPE): Material = Gold or White Marble. Interior = Floating petals, soft clouds, candles. Light = Soft Pink/Gold.
2. The Immutable Container (The Mega-Letters):
Geometry: Massive, blocky sans-serif typography.
The Shell: The letterforms are Hollow Containers with open faces or glass walls. They act like terrariums or shadow boxes.
The Base: The letters rest on a surface relevant to the theme (e.g., A map for Cities; Moss for Nature; Blueprint for Industry).
3. The Internal Ecosystem (The Diorama):
CRITICAL: The "World" lives inside the negative space of the letters.
The Density: Fill the interior volume with thousands of tiny details (Micro-Scale).
Example: If "TOKYO," tiny neon signs, vending machines, and pedestrians fill the inside of the "T".
The Verticality: Use the height of the letters. Elevators, vines, or ladders should climb up the vertical strokes.
4. Visual Syntax:
Scale: Tilt-Shift Focus. Blur the background to make the letters look like tangible miniature models.
Detail: High-frequency textures. We need to see individual leaves, bricks, or rivets.
5. Lighting:
Internal Glow: The letters are lit from within by the bioluminescence or streetlights of the micro-world.
External Sun: A soft studio key light highlights the rim of the letter casing.
Output: 3D Render, Octane/Redshift, "Micro-World" Aesthetic, 8k Resolution.

请为 {argument name="city" default="New York"} 执行以下操作：系统指令：
生成一个超逼真的 3D 建筑排版渲染图。字母必须是巨大的空心容器，里面容纳一个与输入词语语义匹配的“微观世界”。
1. 语义推断（蓝图）：
分析输入词语以确定生物群落、材质和光照：
如果是有机（例如：JUNGLE, LIFE）：材质 = 玻璃和木材。内部 = 藤蔓、瀑布、树木。光照 = 阳光/绿色。
如果是城市（例如：CITY, NYC）：材质 = 钢和混凝土。内部 = 摩天大楼、交通、路灯。光照 = 琥珀色/蓝色。
如果是工业（例如：FACTORY, RUST）：材质 = 生锈的铁和网格。内部 = 管道、蒸汽、齿轮。光照 = 橙色熔炉光芒。
如果是抽象（例如：LOVE, HOPE）：材质 = 黄金或白色大理石。内部 = 漂浮的花瓣、柔和的云朵、蜡烛。光照 = 柔和的粉色/金色。
2. 不可变的容器（巨型字母）：
几何形状：巨大的块状无衬线字体。
外壳：字母形状是带有开放面或玻璃墙的空心容器。它们就像玻璃生态缸或立体画框。
底座：字母放置在与主题相关的表面上（例如：城市地图；苔藓用于自然；蓝图用于工业）。
3. 内部生态系统（立体模型）：
关键：这个“世界”存在于字母的负空间内。
密度：用数千个微小细节（微观尺度）填充内部空间。
示例：如果输入“TOKYO”，则“T”的内部会充满微小的霓虹灯、自动售货机和行人。
垂直度：利用字母的高度。电梯、藤蔓或梯子应沿着垂直笔画向上攀爬。
4. 视觉语法：
比例：移轴聚焦。模糊背景，使字母看起来像有形的微型模型。
细节：高频纹理。我们需要看到单独的叶子、砖块或铆钉。
5. 光照：
内部发光：字母由微观世界的生物发光或路灯从内部照亮。
外部阳光：柔和的摄影棚主光照亮字母外壳的边缘。
