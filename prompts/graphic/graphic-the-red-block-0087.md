# 正文 prompt
Create a bold Swiss-modernist event poster for a live AI and web design sprint, portrait orientation, with a warm off-white paper background, stark black typography, and vivid red accent blocks. The composition is asymmetrical and editorial, with a strong grid and generous negative space. In the top left, place a small red uppercase line of text reading 【LIVE APRIL 27–30】, followed by a thin horizontal black rule stretching toward the right. Below it, set an oversized stacked headline in ultra-bold black sans serif, left aligned, reading 【The AI Web Design Sprint】, broken across 4 lines with dramatic scale so the words dominate the left half of the poster. In the upper right quadrant, add 1 large solid red rectangle as a graphic block. Beneath that red block, place a compact black uppercase subhead in 3 lines: 【4 DAYS. REAL PROJECTS. SHIP TOGETHER.】. In the lower middle, include 2 cropped grayscale portrait photos of men from the chest up, side by side, documentary style, slightly overlapping the typography area; the left man has dark slicked-back hair and a short beard, the right man wears a dark baseball cap with embroidered lettering and has facial hair. Obscure both faces with 2 vertical soft-edged gray censor rectangles centered over each face. Along the right side, create a vertical feature column with 4 black square icons, each separated by thin horizontal divider lines and paired with uppercase labels. The 4 items are: a starburst icon with the label "CREATIVE COLLISION", a connected-nodes icon with the label "AI-POWERED WORKFLOWS", a code brackets icon with the label "CODE IN MOTION", and a terminal prompt icon with the label "REAL-TIME DESIGN". Near the bottom, add a full-width footer separated by a thin horizontal black rule. Inside the footer, create 4 text blocks divided by slim vertical lines: block 1 says "BUILD FAST. BREAK RULES. SHARE LOUD.", block 2 lists "MINIMAL PLAYGROUND & AGENCY 976" with a small red line beneath reading "LIVE ON THE WEB", block 3 lists "CYBERPUNK CAFE SURFERS (LATE SET)" and below it "4 NIGHTS OF CHAOS & CODE" and then "THE SOUTH SIDE DIY", block 4 lists ticket info as "TICKETS $10 ADV $12 DOS". On the far lower right edge, add vertical red text reading 【CHICAGO, IL】. Use a clean neo-grotesk typeface similar to Helvetica or Akzidenz-Grotesk, heavy weight for the headline, condensed uppercase for supporting text, crisp alignment, subtle paper texture, and a high-contrast black-red-cream palette. The overall look should feel like a contemporary design-conference poster mixed with punk tech culture and brutalist editorial branding.
