# 正文 prompt
Draw {"visual subject" "[VISUAL]"} as if created by a cheerful 6-year-old on white notebook paper. Use chunky wax-crayon strokes, wobbly outlines, and simple flat shapes. Colors should be bright, bold, and slightly outside the lines, with visible pressure marks and small smudges. Perspective is innocent: objects can float, and size is based on importance, not realism. Faces are dots with curved smiles; hands are mitten blobs; animals are round and friendly. Add playful extras like a sun in a corner, puffy clouds, stars, and hearts. Include uneven scribble shading, a few tiny doodles in the margins, and one or two hand-written labels in childlike spelling.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
画一个 {"visual subject" "[VISUAL]"}，就像一个开朗的 6 岁孩子在白色笔记本纸上画的那样。使用粗蜡笔笔触、摇晃的轮廓和简单的扁平形状。颜色应明亮、大胆，略微超出线条，带有明显的按压痕迹和小污迹。透视是天真的：物体可以漂浮，大小取决于重要性，而非写实。脸是带弧形微笑的点；手是连指手套状的团块；动物是圆润友好的。添加俏皮的额外元素，如角落里的太阳、蓬松的云朵、星星和爱心。包括不均匀的涂鸦阴影、页边空白处的一两个小涂鸦，以及一两个用稚嫩拼写手写的标签。
