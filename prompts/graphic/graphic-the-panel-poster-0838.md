# 正文 prompt
A dark tech-comparison poster on a black background with a subtle gray square grid, showing two vertically oriented infographic images side by side for an AI image generation comparison. At the top, place two large bold yellow headings: “GPT image 2” above the left panel and “Nano Banana” above the right panel. The left panel is an elegant antique-style Hanfu reference poster on warm aged paper, with a large Chinese calligraphy title at the top reading “汉服·解构图鉴”, smaller subtitle text beneath, and a full-body woman in pale traditional Hanfu standing in the center, her face intentionally blurred. Surround her with many small labeled diagram blocks, fabric swatches, embroidery closeups, accessory notes, silhouette studies, and fine Chinese annotations in a scholarly museum-poster layout. Use beige, cream, light gray-blue, and faded ink tones, giving the panel a refined archival aesthetic. The right panel is a cleaner modern infographic poster on light beige paper with a bold Chinese title across the top reading “中国传统汉服：大明朝服解构图谱”. In the center, show a bright red Ming-style robe drawn as a technical exploded garment diagram with multiple callout arrows and labeled parts. Around it, include material samples, decorative pattern examples, a horizontal row of 6 circular color swatches, construction details, and a bottom strip with 4 small human figure diagrams showing wearing sequence or silhouette comparison. Make the right poster feel more graphic-design driven, with sharper contrast and more vivid red accents. At the bottom of the overall image, add a large rounded black text box with a thin white outline spanning most of the width. Attach a yellow rounded label tab on its upper-left corner containing the Chinese word “提示词” in bold black text. Inside the black box, place a dense paragraph of white Chinese text describing the test prompt for generating a Hanfu-themed decomposition chart, resembling a screenshot quote block. The whole composition should look like a social media comparison graphic, centered, cleanly aligned, high resolution, with strong contrast between the dark background and the two poster panels.
