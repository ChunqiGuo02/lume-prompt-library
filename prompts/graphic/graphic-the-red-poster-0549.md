# 正文 prompt
A bold vintage propaganda-style poster with a limited red, black, and cream palette, printed on slightly textured aged paper. The composition shows 2 women in a dramatic action scene across the center: on the left, a woman with long wavy red hair in a white martial arts gi performs a very high side kick; on the right, a curvy woman in a black cowboy hat and a black lace bodysuit recoils and raises her arms defensively as the kick strikes her face. Use heavy black shadows, sharp angular shapes, screenprint-like ink coverage, and a graphic mid-century pulp poster aesthetic. Behind them, place a large diagonal red geometric banner spanning from upper left to lower right, plus 2 black speed lines on the left and 3 white speed lines on the right to emphasize motion. In the upper right corner, include a circular emblem with a stylized black-and-white screaming monkey or ape face inside a red disk. At the bottom, add oversized condensed block typography in an aggressive poster layout: the first line reads 【SCOTTISH RECIPE:】 in red and black, and the second line reads 【MINCE AND TATTIES】 in very large red, white, and black letters over a black diagonal banner. Keep the overall look satirical, confrontational, retro, and highly graphic, like a fake exploitation movie poster or anarchic punk zine cover.
