# 正文 prompt
A clean minimalist tech logo composition on a light gray background, wide horizontal layout. On the left, show a stacked cluster of 4 overlapping Microsoft Office-style app icons with soft gradients, rounded corners, subtle depth, and glossy modern vector shading. The front icon is a large blue square tile with a smaller raised blue panel containing a bold white capital P. Behind and slightly above it, place an orange presentation icon with a white pie-chart symbol. Behind the blue icon to the right, place a white document page icon with a folded blue corner and 4 horizontal blue text lines. At the back on the right, place a green spreadsheet icon with a white grid. Arrange the 4 icons so they overlap tightly and read as a unified hybrid Office app mark. On the right side, place the product name in a clean sans-serif font: small black text reading Microsoft above a large multicolor wordmark reading 【PowerWordEx】. Color the word in segmented Microsoft-style hues: warm orange-red for Power, deep blue for Word, and teal-green for Ex. Center the whole composition vertically, keep generous whitespace, and render it as polished corporate branding mockup, crisp vector illustration, soft shadowing, no extra objects.

## 中文译文（源站提供）
一张简洁的极简主义企业宣传图，背景为极浅的灰色，水平居中。在左侧，创建一个由 4 个重叠的 Microsoft Office 风格应用图标组成的 3D 光泽堆叠，图标采用圆角设计并带有柔和阴影：最前方是一个巨大的蓝色 PowerPoint 图标，其中包含一个带有粗体白色大写字母 P 的凸起蓝色小方块；其后是一个带有右上角折页和 3 行蓝色水平文本的白蓝色 Word 文档图标；再后是一个带有白色网格的绿色 Excel 表格图标；最后是一个带有浅色饼图的橙色演示文稿 Slides 图标。这些图标应紧密堆叠成一个紧凑的集群，具有现代平滑的渐变、微妙的反射和柔和的深度感。在图标集群的右侧，放置黑色的“Microsoft”字样，使用简洁的无衬线字体，其下方是一个醒目的大标题“PowerWordEx”，将 3 个产品名称在视觉上合并为一个词：“Power”采用橙色到红色的渐变，“Word”采用深蓝色渐变，“Ex”采用青色到蓝绿色的渐变。保持排版清晰平衡，留白充足，呈现出精致的科技品牌美感，并采用宽幅横幅式的构图。
