# 正文 prompt
Create a luxurious Japanese magazine-style PR editorial spread for a publishing and branding service, designed as a two-page brochure layout. The left half is a dark, cinematic portrait scene in a sophisticated office lounge: a seated Japanese businessman in a navy bespoke suit, white shirt, dark tie, white pocket square, and wristwatch sits in a leather chair with one hand near his chin, posed thoughtfully beside a glossy wooden table. His face is intentionally obscured with a soft rectangular blur. The lighting is moody and warm, with reflections from tall windows and a premium executive atmosphere. On this dark left page, place large elegant serif headline text in English reading 【PUBLISHING BRANDING】, stacked in two lines near the upper left in muted gold. Below it, add multiple lines of Japanese copy in white, including a larger subheadline and several short body paragraphs arranged vertically. Near the lower left, include a bordered dark box containing exactly 4 checklist items, each preceded by a check mark, with Japanese text. Put a small page number "02" in the bottom-left corner. The right half is a clean white editorial information page with refined minimalist typography and generous spacing. At the top, place a large Japanese headline in black, aligned upper center-right, with two short Japanese body text blocks beneath it; on the upper far right include a small photo of exactly 4 stacked hardcover books and a glass vase near a bright window with greenery. Across the middle, add a centered section title in Japanese for five benefits, followed by exactly 5 numbered columns labeled 1 through 5, each with a short Japanese heading and several lines of smaller explanatory Japanese text. Beneath that, add another centered Japanese section title for workflow, followed by exactly 4 step blocks laid out horizontally: STEP 01 with a speech bubble icon, STEP 02 with a pencil icon, STEP 03 with an open book icon, STEP 04 with a megaphone icon. Each step includes the English word STEP plus a two-digit number, a short Japanese subtitle, and a few lines of smaller Japanese description, with small chevrons between steps. Along the bottom of the right page, place exactly 3 rectangular photos side by side: a city-view office meeting table scene, a close-up of a hand writing with a pen, and a stack of books near an open book. Under these images, add a prominent Japanese closing statement in a serif style on the lower left, and a smaller Japanese paragraph on the lower right. Put page number "03" in the bottom-right corner. Use a premium palette of deep navy, black, warm brown, ivory, and muted gold, with a balance of editorial luxury, corporate trust, and high-end publishing aesthetics. The overall composition should feel like a real magazine advertisement or consulting brochure, polished, photorealistic, elegant, and credible.

为出版与品牌服务创作一份奢华的日式杂志风格公关编辑排版，设计为双页宣传册布局。左半部分为深色电影质感的肖像场景，背景设在精致的办公休息室：一位身着海军蓝定制西装、白衬衫、深色领带、白色口袋巾并佩戴腕表的日本商务人士，坐在皮椅上，单手托腮，姿态沉思，身旁是一张光亮的木质桌子。他的面部经过柔和的矩形模糊处理。灯光氛围感强且温暖，伴有落地窗的倒影，营造出高级行政氛围。在此深色左页上，放置醒目优雅的衬线英文标题 【PUBLISHING BRANDING】，以哑光金色分两行堆叠在左上方。下方添加多行白色日文文案，包括一个较大的副标题和几段垂直排列的简短正文。左下角附近包含一个带边框的深色方框，内含 4 个带勾选标记的清单项，并配有日文文本。左下角放置页码“02”。右半部分为简洁的白色编辑信息页，采用精致的极简主义排版和宽裕的留白。顶部中央偏右处放置一个醒目的黑色日文大标题，下方配有两个简短的日文正文块；右上角包含一张小图，展示 4 本堆叠的精装书和一个靠近明亮窗户及绿植的玻璃花瓶。页面中部添加一个居中的日文五大优势标题，下方排列 5 个编号列（1 至 5），每列包含简短的日文标题及几行较小的日文说明文字。下方添加另一个居中的日文工作流程标题，随后横向排列 4 个步骤块：STEP 01 配有对话气泡图标，STEP 02 配有铅笔图标，STEP 03 配有打开的书本图标，STEP 04 配有扩音器图标。每个步骤包含英文单词 STEP 及两位数编号、简短的日文副标题和几行较小的日文描述，步骤之间以小箭头连接。右页底部并排放置 3 张矩形照片：城市景观办公会议桌场景、手持钢笔书写的特写，以及靠近打开书本的一叠书籍。在这些图片下方，左侧添加一段醒目的衬线风格日文结语，右侧添加一段较小的日文段落。右下角放置页码“03”。采用深海军蓝、黑、暖棕、象牙白和哑光金的高级配色，平衡编辑奢华感、企业信任度与高端出版美学。整体构图应呈现出真实杂志广告或咨询手册的质感，精致、写实、优雅且具有公信力。
