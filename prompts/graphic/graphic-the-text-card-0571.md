# 正文 prompt
A single vertically oriented fantasy trading card in a richly ornamented high-fantasy style, designed like a premium collectible TCG card with fully legible printed text and icons. The card has an elaborate dark navy and antique gold frame with filigree corners, embossed metallic trim, and a painterly realistic illustration in the center. At the top center, large serif title text reads 【VARIAN THE AZUREMAGE】, with a smaller subtitle beneath reading 【HUMAN WIZARD】. In the top left corner, place a circular blue mana-like emblem containing a white swirling arcane symbol. In the top right corner, place a small heraldic banner panel with 2 gold icons stacked vertically: an arcane starburst above an open book. Along the left edge, create a vertical stat column with exactly 4 icon-and-number rows on dark blue plaques: a sword icon with the number 2, a shield icon with the number 1, a red heart icon with the number 3, and a boot icon with the number 2. The central artwork shows a male human wizard in a cathedral-like magical interior filled with blue light and faint candles, wearing layered royal-blue robes and cloak with intricate gold embroidery, gilded armor accents, leather belts, pouches, scroll cases, and jeweled ornaments. He has dark wavy medium-length hair and a dramatic heroic pose, gripping an ornate staff in his left hand; the staff is black-gold with branching prongs and a glowing blue crystal near the top. His right arm is extended outward, conjuring a luminous spiral galaxy of blue arcane energy, star-like particles, and trailing magical ribbons that swirl around his hand. The mood is majestic, mystical, and cinematic, with intense sapphire highlights, deep shadows, painterly detail, and premium fantasy card-game illustration quality. At the bottom, add a parchment-colored rules text box with ornate corners and 2 abilities, each preceded by a blue arcane symbol bullet. The first ability title is bold serif text: ARCANE BOLT, followed by the rules text: When Varian attacks, he deals 2 damage to any target. The second ability title is bold serif text: MANA SURGE, followed by the rules text: Once per turn, you may pay 1 mana (with a blue water-drop mana icon) to draw a card. Beneath the abilities, add an italic flavor quote in blue-gray text: “Knowledge is the truest power. Master it, and the world is yours to shape.” In the lower left corner, place a large blue faceted gem badge with the number 【3】. In the lower right corner, place a circular gold-framed open book icon. Along the bottom border, centered in gold serif text on a blue strip, write: 【ALLY • HUMAN • WIZARD】. Make the whole card look like a polished professional TCG product mockup, crisp typography, balanced layout, ornate fantasy UI, and fully readable English text.
