# 正文 prompt
A vintage travel poster for 【KYOTO】, Japan, designed in a nostalgic mid-century tourism style with a faded paper texture, softly worn edges, and warm sun-aged colors. Use a vertical poster layout with rounded corners and a cream border. At the top, place the large headline 【KYOTO】 in oversized elegant red serif letters, with “JAPAN” centered below in dark blue serif type and thin decorative divider lines. Add an italic tagline beneath it reading 【Timeless Beauty. Endless Memories.】. The main image should show a dramatic low-angle view of a traditional five-story Japanese pagoda in vivid vermilion red with dark wooden eaves and black railings, standing on a stone base with a wooden walkway in the foreground. Surround the pagoda with green wooded hills, a bright blue sky, and soft cream-colored clouds. On the left side, include a vertical red label with white Japanese characters reading “京都、日本”. Also add a blooming cherry blossom branch entering from the left, with clusters of pale pink flowers. In the upper right corner, include a postage stamp featuring a pagoda silhouette in muted red tones, partially overlapped by a circular postal cancellation mark reading “KYOTO JAPAN” with the kanji “京都” in the center and a few wavy postmark lines extending rightward. At the bottom, add a centered vintage footer slogan in serif capitals reading 【DISCOVER TRADITION. EMBRACE KYOTO.】. The overall look should feel romantic, collectible, slightly distressed, and like a classic Japanese tourism print from the 1950s or 1960s.
