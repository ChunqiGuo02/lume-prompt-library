# 正文 prompt
A realistic editorial product photo of a glossy magazine lying diagonally on a warm medium-brown wooden table, viewed from above at a slight angle. The magazine dominates the frame and has a deep cobalt-blue cover with a large elegant serif masthead reading 【Convergence】 across the top. On the left side of the cover, a huge stacked white sans-serif headline reads 【CHATGPT IMAGES 2.0】. The main cover image is a highly detailed green chameleon in close-up profile facing left, wearing a tan cowboy hat with a brown leather band and metal stud, creating a surreal but polished magazine-cover concept. Add small editorial cover lines in white text beneath and along the bottom, arranged in neat columns like a technology or design magazine, with tiny issue details in the upper right including a price and date. The magazine should look physically printed, with visible page thickness, subtle edge wear, and realistic reflections on the glossy cover. Surrounding environment: soft leafy shadows fall across the tabletop from the upper left, a clear faceted drinking glass is partially visible on the right edge, an orange-red plate peeks in from the top right corner, a pale yellow sticky note in the lower right reads 【SOTA】 in handwritten uppercase letters, and a hint of a green houseplant appears in the upper left. Use warm natural afternoon sunlight, cinematic shadows, crisp focus, and photorealistic materials for a premium lifestyle desk scene.
